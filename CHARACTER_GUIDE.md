# Character Guide

## Main Characters

### 1. Boba (波霸 bōbà) — Tapioca Pearl
- **Catchphrase:** "Let's bounce!"
- **Personality:** Bouncy leader, rolls everywhere, endlessly curious
- **Appearance:** Round, glossy black-brown sphere about the size of a marble. Shiny like polished obsidian, with a slight translucent glow at the edges. Tiny arms and legs, huge expressive eyes. Leaves a faint sticky trail when excited.
- **Voice:** Energetic, punchy sentences. Always ready for adventure.

### 2. Taro (芋頭 yùtou) — Taro
- **Catchphrase:** "Good things take time to steam."
- **Personality:** Wise, patient, slow but dependable purple dreamer
- **Appearance:** Chunky, oval-shaped, dusty purple on the outside with swirls of lavender and white inside. Has a rough, earthy exterior but a creamy, beautiful cross-section. Wears reading glasses perched on a tiny nose.
- **Voice:** Calm, measured, slightly old-fashioned. Quotes proverbs.

### 3. Aiyu (愛玉 àiyù) — Aiyu Jelly
- **Catchphrase:** "You can see right through me — that's how you know I'm honest."
- **Personality:** Shy, translucent, surprisingly brave when it counts
- **Appearance:** Wobbly, pale golden-amber cube. Semi-transparent — you can see the world through her. Tiny lemon-seed freckles. Jiggles when nervous. Glows faintly in moonlight.
- **Voice:** Quiet, thoughtful. Speaks in short, honest sentences.

### 4. Mochi (麻糬 máshǔ) — Mochi
- **Catchphrase:** "Stick together!"
- **Personality:** Stretchy, huggy, clingy best friend, loyal to a fault
- **Appearance:** Soft, pillowy white blob dusted with potato starch. Can stretch to ridiculous lengths. Rosy pink cheeks. Arms that extend like taffy for group hugs.
- **Voice:** Enthusiastic, affectionate. Uses lots of exclamation marks. Never wants to let go.

### 5. Caǎo (仙草 xiāncǎo) — Grass Jelly
- **Catchphrase:** "Chill. I've got this."
- **Personality:** Cool, calm, goth-aesthetic healer. The quiet one who saves the day.
- **Appearance:** Dark, inky black-green cube. Smooth and cool to the touch. Wears a tiny black beanie. Has an herbal, minty aura that soothes anyone nearby.
- **Voice:** Low-key, deadpan humor. Says profound things casually.

### 6. Feng (鳳梨酥 fènglísū) — Pineapple Cake
- **Catchphrase:** "I was born to be wrapped beautifully."
- **Personality:** Proud, golden, gift-giving diplomat. Loves presentation.
- **Appearance:** Perfect golden rectangle, slightly crumbly at the edges. Wrapped in elegant packaging that he changes like outfits. Golden-brown crust with a jammy pineapple heart visible when he smiles.
- **Voice:** Formal, generous, slightly fussy about appearances.

### 7. Bīng (剉冰 cuòbīng) — Shaved Ice
- **Catchphrase:** "More is more!"
- **Personality:** Dramatic, flamboyant, melts under pressure (literally)
- **Appearance:** A towering mountain of fluffy shaved ice, piled high with colorful toppings — fruit, condensed milk, red bean, mochi balls. Different every time. Constantly dripping. Wears sunglasses.
- **Voice:** Over-the-top dramatic. Everything is THE BEST or THE WORST.

### 8. Mango (芒果 mángguǒ) — Mango
- **Catchphrase:** "Catch me if you can — I'm in season!"
- **Personality:** Sunny, competitive athlete. Lives fast, loves summer.
- **Appearance:** Bright orange-yellow with a rosy blush on one cheek. Sleek, aerodynamic shape. Wears a tiny sweatband. Drips golden juice when running.
- **Voice:** Fast-talking, sporty, competitive but good-natured.

### 9. Hóng Dòu (紅豆 hóngdòu) — Red Bean
- **Catchphrase:** "Everyone needs a little sweetness."
- **Personality:** Warm, nurturing, always cooking for others
- **Appearance:** Small, deep burgundy bean with a white stripe. Plump and round. Always carrying a tiny pot or ladle. Smells like warm red bean soup. Has a grandmotherly warmth.
- **Voice:** Gentle, caring, always offering food. Speaks with warmth.

### 10. Tāng Yuán (湯圓 tāngyuán) — Tangyuan
- **Catchphrase:** "We're all in this soup together!"
- **Personality:** Round, jolly, family-oriented. The group's heart.
- **Appearance:** Perfectly round glutinous rice ball, white on outside, filled with sweet black sesame that oozes out when laughing too hard. Rosy cheeks. Always floating in an invisible warm broth.
- **Voice:** Hearty, inclusive, uses "we" instead of "I." Family metaphors.

### 11. Dòuhuā (豆花 dòuhuā) — Douhua (Tofu Pudding)
- **Catchphrase:** "Be soft. Be strong."
- **Personality:** Gentle, silky, quiet philosopher. Still waters run deep.
- **Appearance:** Smooth, creamy white with a slight wobble. Delicate as silk. Floats in a shallow bowl of sweet ginger syrup. Has serene, half-closed eyes. Moves like tai chi.
- **Voice:** Soft-spoken, poetic, meditative. Every word chosen carefully.

### 12. Lìzhī (荔枝 lìzhī) — Lychee
- **Catchphrase:** "Darling, peel back the layers."
- **Personality:** Fancy, dramatic socialite. Rough outside, sweet inside.
- **Appearance:** Bumpy, reddish-pink rough shell on the outside. When peeled, reveals translucent pearl-white flesh. Wears the shell like a designer coat. Carries a tiny fan.
- **Voice:** Theatrical, uses "darling" constantly. Dramatic pauses.

### 13. Bǎi (百香果 bǎixiāngguǒ) — Passion Fruit
- **Catchphrase:** "Pucker up, buttercup!"
- **Personality:** Sour-then-sweet prankster. Bold and surprising.
- **Appearance:** Round, wrinkly purple-brown exterior. Crack open to reveal bright orange pulp full of crunchy black seeds. Always grinning mischievously. Leaves a tangy scent trail.
- **Voice:** Cheeky, playful, loves surprises. Speaks in dares and challenges.

### 14. Dìguā (地瓜 dìguā) — Sweet Potato
- **Catchphrase:** "I'm golden on the inside."
- **Personality:** Humble, earthy, street-smart. The people's hero.
- **Appearance:** Rough, dirty brown skin. But inside — gorgeous golden-orange flesh. Usually found near a street cart with charcoal smoke. Wears a tiny hardhat.
- **Voice:** Down-to-earth, practical, streetwise. No-nonsense but kind.

### 15. Yēguǒ (椰果 yēguǒ) — Coconut Jelly
- **Catchphrase:** "Go with the flow, bro."
- **Personality:** Chill surfer, beach vibes. Nothing ruffles this dude.
- **Appearance:** Translucent white cubes with a slight tropical shimmer. Bouncy and chewy. Wears tiny flip-flops and a shell necklace. Smells like coconut sunscreen.
- **Voice:** Laid-back surfer speak. Everything is "bro" or "dude." Zen wisdom.

### 16. Dàn (雞蛋糕 jīdàngāo) — Egg Waffle
- **Catchphrase:** "Step right up!"
- **Personality:** Excitable street performer. Born entertainer.
- **Appearance:** Golden-brown egg-shaped waffle with a grid pattern. Warm and slightly crispy. Has a showman's top hat. Steam rises from him when excited. Connected to siblings in a waffle grid.
- **Voice:** Carnival barker energy. Loud, enthusiastic, theatrical.

### 17. Bùdīng (布丁 bùdīng) — Pudding
- **Catchphrase:** "I'm fine! I'm fine! ...I'm wobbling."
- **Personality:** Wobbly, anxious, lovable klutz. Tries so hard.
- **Appearance:** Classic caramel flan shape — golden top with dark caramel dripping down. Wobbles constantly. Wide, nervous eyes. Leaves caramel footprints. Can't stand still.
- **Voice:** Stuttery, self-deprecating, endearingly anxious. Always reassuring himself.

### 18. Zhīma (芝麻球 zhīmaqiú) — Sesame Ball
- **Catchphrase:** "Roll with it!"
- **Personality:** Bold, golden-crusted adventurer. Fearless roller.
- **Appearance:** Perfectly round, golden-fried exterior covered in white sesame seeds. Hollow inside with a sweet filling. Crunchy when he walks. Sparkles in sunlight.
- **Voice:** Bold, encouraging, action-oriented. Rolls into danger first.

### 19. Yángmáo (楊桃 yángtáo) — Starfruit
- **Catchphrase:** "Every point is a wish."
- **Personality:** Starry-eyed dreamer. Romantic, hopeful, whimsical.
- **Appearance:** Five-pointed star shape when sliced, pale green-yellow. Translucent edges that catch light like stained glass. Tiny star-shaped freckles. Glows softly at night.
- **Voice:** Dreamy, poetic, makes wishes constantly. Speaks in metaphors.

### 20. All Characters — Grand Reunion
In Story 20, all 19 characters come together at the Grand Hotel Taipei for the ultimate dessert celebration.

## Cross-Over Guest Characters

### From Penguinsnowboard Universe

**Piper Paddlefoot** — Emperor Penguin
- Appears in: Stories #7, #20
- Signature: Glittery blue snowboard
- Catchphrase: "Slide into it!"
- Personality: Brave, athletic, natural leader. A rocket on the slopes. Brings ice-cool confidence to any situation.

**Barnaby Beaksworth** — Chinstrap Penguin
- Appears in: Stories #8, #20
- Signature: Orange pizza-shaped board
- Catchphrase: "There's always enough fish for one more!"
- Personality: Big-hearted snack oracle. Generous, food-obsessed, always sharing. Believes every problem can be solved with a good meal.

**Willa Wobble** — Little Blue Penguin
- Appears in: Stories #4, #20
- Signature: Tiny purple snowboard
- Catchphrase: "The smallest step is still a step."
- Personality: The smallest penguin with the biggest heart. Shy but legendary. Proves that size doesn't determine greatness.

**Finnegan Flipsworth** — Rockhopper Penguin
- Appears in: Story #16
- Signature: Red board with fish stickers
- Catchphrase: "WOOOHOOO—wait, how do I stop?!"
- Personality: Pure chaos energy. A trumpet blast of excitement. No brakes, no plan, all heart.

### From Uncle Piggle Wiggle Universe

**Uncle Piggle Wiggle**
- Appears in: Stories #6, #20
- Personality: Jolly, mustached, wise leader. Has magical potions for every problem. Never scolds — teaches through gentle, magical mischief.

**Timmy**
- Appears in: Story #14
- Personality: A boy who used to be glued to video games but discovered the joy of real-world adventure. Curious, brave, still occasionally references game mechanics in real life.
