# Storyboard: Story 22 — Grass Jelly and the Stinky Tofu Demon

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, warm ochre and gold tones, cozy night-market atmosphere, soft edges

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Caǎo (dark black-green cube, tiny black beanie, calm half-lidded eyes) and Xiǎo Chòu (round pudgy demon toddler, crooked horns, enormous smile, visible stink lines) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots for consistency.

---

## Shot 1 — THE SMELL (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A dark inky black-green cube of grass jelly wearing a tiny black beanie sitting on the steps of an old brick building on a narrow Taiwanese old street. Visible stink/aroma waves drifting through the air. Traditional shopfronts with Chinese signage, warm lantern light. Whimsical storybook illustration, warm ochre tones.`

**Narration:**
> "The smell hit Caǎo from six blocks away. Now, Caǎo was not the kind of dessert ingredient who got worked up about things. Surprise birthday party? 'Cool.' Typhoon? 'Whatever.' But this smell was extraordinary."

---

## Shot 2 — FINDING XIǍO CHÒU (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny round pudgy demon toddler the size of a rice cooker, with crooked stubby horns, enormous watery eyes, and visible stink lines, hiding behind wooden crates near a tofu factory. She's crying big snotty tears. A dark grass jelly cube in a beanie approaches calmly. Old Taiwanese alley setting, warm lighting, storybook style.`

**Narration:**
> "Behind a stack of wooden crates near the old tofu factory, he found the source. It was a demon. The smallest, roundest, most pathetic-looking demon Caǎo had ever seen. And she was crying."

---

## Shot 3 — "I DON'T HAVE A NOSE" (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Close-up of a dark grass jelly cube with half-lidded calm eyes and a tiny black beanie, facing a sniffling round demon toddler with crooked horns. The demon looks hopeful and surprised. Simple warm background. Cute character interaction, storybook illustration style.`

**Narration:**
> "'Please don't run away! I know I smell bad!' 'I wasn't running away. I was standing here. Standing is, like, the opposite of running.' 'You're not going to hold your nose?' 'I don't have a nose.'"

---

## Shot 4 — THE WALKING TOUR BEGINS (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A dark grass jelly cube in a black beanie walking coolly down a narrow old Taiwanese street alongside a tiny round demon toddler who looks up with wonder. Both sides of the street lined with stinky tofu stalls, steam rising, shop signs in Chinese characters, red lanterns. Warm, atmospheric storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "'You're standing in the middle of Shenkeng Old Street. You know what this entire town smells like? Exactly like you. This whole street is literally famous for smelling like you. You're not a problem. You're on-brand.'"

---

## Shot 5 — FRIED STINKY TOFU STALL (1:10–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny round demon toddler pressing her face against a shop window display of golden crispy fried stinky tofu with pickled cabbage. The shop owner inside gives a thumbs up and smiles. A grass jelly cube stands nearby looking cool. Warm golden lighting, traditional Taiwanese food stall, storybook style.`

**Narration:**
> "At every shop, Caǎo stopped and pointed. 'Fried stinky tofu. Classic.' The shop owner took one sniff of Xiǎo Chòu and gave a thumbs up. 'Wow, you smell amazing! Like our premium aged batch!'"

---

## Shot 6 — THE FOOD TOUR MONTAGE (1:25–1:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Montage-style split scenes: grilled stinky tofu on sticks, a bubbling hot pot with tofu, and a cup of stinky tofu ice cream. A tiny demon toddler and a grass jelly cube visit each one, the demon looking increasingly amazed and happy. Warm Taiwanese old street setting, storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "'Grilled stinky tofu on a stick. Street food royalty.' 'Stinky tofu hot pot — the single greatest cold-weather food known to humankind or demonkind.' And then... 'Stinky tofu ice cream.' 'That can't be real.' 'This is Taiwan. We put everything in ice cream.'"

---

## Shot 7 — XIǍO CHÒU'S FIRST GIGGLE (1:45–2:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny round demon toddler with crooked horns giggling for the first time, eyes squeezed shut with joy, sitting on a stone bench next to a calm dark grass jelly cube in a beanie. Warm old street background, golden lantern light, heartwarming storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "Xiǎo Chòu giggled for the first time. It sounded like a tiny bell being rung inside a sock. 'The stink isn't the problem,' Caǎo said. 'The stink is the invitation. The brave ones follow the smell.'"

---

## Shot 8 — THE DEMON STAMPEDE (2:00–2:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A massive crowd of demon children of all sizes — tiny ones, medium ones, and one the size of a refrigerator — stampeding down a narrow Taiwanese old street, sniffing the air like excited puppies. Horns, tails, bat wings, one appears to be on fire. Comedic chaos, warm storybook illustration, exaggerated expressions.`

**Narration:**
> "'WHAT IS THAT INCREDIBLE SMELL?' bellowed the refrigerator-sized one. 'FOLLOW THE STINK!' they all screamed in unison. And they charged. Directly toward Xiǎo Chòu."

---

## Shot 9 — "OWN IT" (2:15–2:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A dark grass jelly cube in a beanie standing calmly in front of a panicking tiny demon toddler, facing an approaching horde of excited demon children. The grass jelly looks completely unfazed. Old street setting, dramatic but comedic lighting, storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "'Caǎo! They're coming! What do I do?' Caǎo brushed off his beanie. He looked at the stampeding horde with total, unshakeable calm. 'You stand right there. And you own it.'"

---

## Shot 10 — THE PERFECT PAIRING (2:30–2:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A golden plate of crispy fried stinky tofu with pickled cabbage, and next to it a dark grass jelly cube sliding coolly into position beside the tofu. The plate glows with warm light. A tiny demon toddler watches in amazement. Close-up food shot, storybook illustration, golden and dark green color contrast.`

**Narration:**
> "He slid himself onto the plate. Right next to the tofu. A perfect dark cube of herbal grass jelly beside golden fried stinky tofu. The perfect pairing. 'Completing the combo,' he said calmly."

---

## Shot 11 — THE FEAST (2:45–3:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A joyful chaotic feast scene: dozens of demon children of all sizes devouring plates of stinky tofu and grass jelly, cheering, crying happy tears. A tiny demon toddler with crooked horns is hoisted on the shoulders of a massive refrigerator-sized demon, laughing with pure joy. Shenkeng Old Street glowing with warm lantern light. Celebratory storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "They ate EVERYTHING. And Caǎo was devoured in exactly three bites. From inside the demon's stomach, a faint calm voice: 'Yeah. I completed the perfect flavor combination. No big deal. Pretty comfortable in here, actually.'"

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:05–3:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny round demon toddler with crooked horns being paraded down a lantern-lit old street on the shoulders of cheering demon children, laughing with pure joy, stink lines radiating proudly. Shop owners wave and toss free samples. Warm, triumphant, dreamy storybook illustration fading into soft moonlight.`

**Narration:**
> "She never felt stinky again. The stink was never the problem. The stink was the invitation. And the brave ones? They always follow the smell. Goodnight, you beautifully weird little human. Goodnight."

---

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Caǎo = dark black-green cube, tiny black beanie, calm half-lidded eyes, herbal green glow. Xiǎo Chòu = round pudgy demon, crooked horns, enormous eyes, visible stink aura.
- **Art style:** Keep "warm ochre and gold tones, old Taiwanese street" consistent across all shots.
- **Audio:** Caǎo's narration should be deadpan/calm. Consider a chill, low voice for his lines.
- **Music:** Something lo-fi and chill, matching Caǎo's vibe. Shift to upbeat for the feast scene.
- **Transitions:** Slow dissolves for the walking tour, quick cuts for the stampede/feast.
