# Storyboard: Story 23 — Egg Waffle and the Demon Chicken Festival

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, neon-drenched night market vibes, warm golden and red tones, dramatic theatrical lighting

> **Tip:** Generate reference images of Dàn (tiny golden-brown egg waffle, top hat, grid pattern, steaming) and Big Wok (squat red demon, three stacked chef hats) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots.

## Shot 3 — THE GLORIOUS JĪPÁI (0:35–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**

**Narration:**
> "If you've never had jīpái, imagine a piece of chicken the size of your FACE. Pounded flat, marinated in five-spice and garlic, deep-fried until golden and crunchy. Eating it while walking through a night market is basically a rite of passage in Taiwan."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Dàn = tiny golden-brown egg waffle, grid pattern, top hat, steam. Big Wok = squat red demon, three stacked chef hats.
- **Energy arc:** Starts high energy, builds through chaos, hits emotional peak at the speech, explosive climax, then soft warm ending.
- **Audio:** Dàn's voice should be LOUD and theatrical for MC scenes, then soft and warm for the farewell. Consider crowd cheering SFX.
- **Music:** Upbeat carnival/night market music for competition scenes, dramatic orchestral swell for the speech/dive, soft lullaby for the closing.
- **Transitions:** Quick, punchy cuts during the cooking chaos. Slow-motion effect for the stage dive. Soft dissolve to closing.
