# Storyboard: Story 06 — Pineapple Cake and the Golden Gift

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, polished gold and crimson palette, fancy mall meets warm classroom, soft edges, generous warm light

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Feng (perfect golden rectangle, slightly crumbly buttery edges, jammy pineapple heart visible when he smiles, in a red gift box with gold ribbon and embossed phoenix logo) and Uncle Piggle Wiggle (top hat, magnificent curling mustache, patchwork coat, carpet bag) in Shots 1 and 4. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — DISPLAY CASE PERFECTION (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A perfect golden pineapple cake rectangle with slightly crumbly buttery edges, sitting proudly inside a polished bakery display case in the Taipei 101 mall. He's wrapped in an elegant red gift box with a gold ribbon and an embossed phoenix logo. Marble floors, jewel-bright bakery lighting. Elegant storybook illustration with gold, red, and cream tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Feng had wrapped himself in his finest gift box — the red one with the gold ribbon and the embossed phoenix logo — and was admiring his reflection in the polished marble floor. 'Exquisite,' he announced. 'Simply exquisite. I was born to be wrapped beautifully.'"

---

## Shot 2 — TAIPEI 101 MALL (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wide establishing shot of the gleaming B1 level of Taipei 101 mall — luxury boutiques, polished marble, the iconic Taipei 101 skyscraper visible through skylights. The fanciest bakery on the floor, rows of golden pineapple cakes arranged like soldiers in display cases. Elegant, polished storybook illustration with crimson and gold accents.`

**Narration:**
> "He lived in the fanciest bakery on the B1 level of Taipei 101 — the shopping center at the base of the tallest building in Taiwan. The display cases gleamed like jewelry stores, and the pineapple cakes were arranged in rows like golden soldiers standing at attention."

---

## Shot 3 — THE DISPLAY PIECE (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A perfect golden pineapple cake in a red gift box sitting alone in the bakery display case at night, the mall closed and dim. He gazes longingly through the glass at the empty corridor. Lonely, contemplative storybook illustration with soft after-hours blue and warm display amber.`

**Narration:**
> "But there was a problem with being the perfect gift: Feng had never been given to anyone. The bakery owner kept him there as the 'sample' — the advertisement. The model. 'I'm a display piece. I'm packaging with nothing inside. I want to be given. To someone who needs a gift.'"

---

## Shot 4 — UNCLE PIGGLE WIGGLE ARRIVES (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A jolly mustachioed gentleman in a top hat and patchwork coat carrying a clinking carpet bag walking through the Taipei 101 mall, his magnificent mustache curled like question marks. Mall vendors wave but he tips his hat and walks past. Magical, warm storybook illustration with rich gold, crimson, and patchwork colors.`

**Narration:**
> "He was a man — at least, he was shaped like a man — wearing a top hat, a magnificent mustache, and a coat covered in patches of every color. He hummed a tune that made everyone who heard it feel inexplicably happy. He stopped in front of Feng's bakery and looked directly at Feng. 'Ah. There you are.'"

---

## Shot 5 — THE PURCHASE (1:10–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Inside the elegant bakery, a top-hatted mustachioed man pointing through the glass at a golden pineapple cake in a red gift box. The salesgirl looks slightly confused but nods. Warm storybook illustration with bakery's warm gold light and rich cream tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'I'd like that one, please. The one in the red box with the gold ribbon.' 'Oh, that's our display sample—' 'That's the one I need.' Something in his voice — kind, certain, impossible to argue with — made the salesgirl shrug and hand over Feng's box."

---

## Shot 6 — UP THE ELEVATORS (1:25–1:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A man with a top hat and patchwork coat carrying a red gift box, riding glass elevators up the Taipei 101 tower past luxury floors, the city of Taipei spreading below like a circuit board of lights. Magical, vertiginous storybook illustration with gold and twilight blue.`

**Narration:**
> "Uncle Piggle Wiggle carried Feng through the mall and up, up, up — past the luxury floors, past the observatory entrance, until they reached the 89th-floor outdoor observation area, where the wind was sharp and the city spread out below like a circuit board made of lights."

## Shot 7 — MEI-LING ON THE BENCH (1:40–2:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A ten-year-old girl named Mei-Ling in a school uniform sitting on a bench at the Taipei 101 observation deck, holding a crumpled paper in both hands. Her eyes are red. Wind in her hair. The dazzling Taipei skyline behind her. Tender storybook illustration with twilight blues and warm city lights.`

**Narration:**
> "Sitting on a bench was a girl. About ten, in a school uniform, holding a crumpled piece of paper. Her eyes were red. 'That's Mei-Ling,' Uncle Piggle Wiggle told Feng. 'Her family just moved to Taipei from Hualien. Tomorrow is her first day at a new school. She doesn't know anyone. She's terrified.'"

## Shot 8 — THE GIFT IS GIVEN (2:00–2:20)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A jolly mustachioed gentleman gently placing a red gift box with gold ribbon on a sad girl's lap, sitting beside her on the Taipei 101 observation deck bench. A small bottle of sparkling golden liquid sits between them. Warm, magical storybook illustration with golden glow and twilight skyline.`

**Narration:**
> "'In my experience, the best way to make a friend is to give them something. Not because you want something back. Just because giving is the bravest thing a person can do.' He placed Feng's gift box on Mei-Ling's lap. She untied the gold ribbon. There was Feng — sitting in his tissue paper nest like a tiny edible jewel."

---

## Shot 9 — UNCLE PIGGLE WIGGLE WALKS AWAY (2:20–2:35)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A man with a top hat, patchwork coat, and carpet bag walking away from the Taipei 101 observation deck into the golden sunset, tipping his hat. The girl Mei-Ling cradles a red gift box, smiling through tears. Magical, warm storybook illustration with deep gold sunset.`

**Narration:**
> "'Don't thank me,' said Uncle Piggle Wiggle, standing and tipping his hat. 'Thank the cake. He's been waiting his whole life for this moment.' And then he was gone — humming that impossible tune, as if jolly mustachioed men delivering pineapple cakes on the 89th floor was the most normal thing in the world."

---

## Shot 10 — THE CLASSROOM OFFER (2:35–2:55)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A bright Taipei elementary school classroom. Mei-Ling at her assigned desk, opening a red gift box with golden pineapple cake inside, offering half to a curious girl at the next desk. Other students glance over. Hopeful, warm storybook illustration with warm classroom light and golden cake glow.`

**Narration:**
> "She sat down at her assigned desk. The girl next to her glanced over, curious. Mei-Ling took a breath. She opened Feng's box. 'Would you like a piece of pineapple cake?' Her voice shook, only a little. 'Is that from the Taipei 101 bakery? I LOVE their pineapple cake!' 'Me too.'"

---

## Shot 11 — BREAKING IN HALF (2:55–3:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A close-up of two girls' hands carefully breaking a golden pineapple cake in half, scattering golden crumbs like tiny fireworks, the pineapple jam interior glowing bright and sweet. Two smiling faces lean in. Warm classroom light, joyful, sweet storybook illustration with gold, jam-amber, and cream tones.`

**Narration:**
> "She broke Feng in half — carefully, reverently, the way you break something precious. Golden crumbs scattered like tiny fireworks. She gave half to the girl. 'I'm Mei-Ling.' 'I'm Xiao-Yú.' They each took a bite at the same time. 'Ōuyīxī!' they said in unison."

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:15–3:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`The Taipei 101 building at sunset, viewed from below, with a single crumpled gold ribbon catching the last light on a wooden bench. Soft city lights glowing. Sleepy, magical, nostalgic storybook illustration with warm gold, amber, and deep blue tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, generous one, may you have the golden heart of Feng. May you know that the best gifts aren't the ones in the fanciest boxes — they're the ones given with the most love. Rest now, wrapped in the warmth of giving. Goodnight, little gift. Goodnight."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Feng = perfect golden rectangle, slightly crumbly buttery edges, jammy pineapple heart visible when smiling, red gift box with embossed phoenix logo and gold ribbon. Uncle Piggle Wiggle = top hat, magnificent curling mustache, patchwork coat, carpet bag clinking with bottles. Mei-Ling = ~10 years old, school uniform, kind sad eyes warming across the story.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, polished gold and crimson palette, generous warm light" in every prompt. Setting shifts: bakery (1-3), mall corridors (4-5), elevator/observation deck (6-9), classroom (10-11), Taipei sunset (12).
- **Audio:** Feng's voice formal, slightly fussy, warming across the story. Uncle Piggle Wiggle's voice booming, warm, mysterious. Mei-Ling's voice small but brave by Shot 10.
- **Music:** Elegant strings for bakery and mall scenes. Magical chime motif when Uncle Piggle Wiggle appears. Tender warm strings for the bench and classroom moments. Lullaby for goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Slow ceremonial dissolves throughout. Long magical fade as Uncle Piggle Wiggle walks away (Shot 9). Hold long on Shot 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 8 (the gift placed) and Shot 11 (breaking in half) deserve longer holds for emotional weight.
