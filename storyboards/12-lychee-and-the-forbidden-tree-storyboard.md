# Storyboard: Story 12 — Lychee and the Forbidden Tree

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, Nantou orchard palette — bumpy pink, deep tropical green, golden afternoon sunlight, hidden pink interior glow — soft edges, dramatic-romantic warmth

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Lìzhī (bumpy reddish-pink lychee with one section of shell peeled back to reveal pearl-white flesh, tiny leaf fan, dramatic theatrical pose) and Xiǎo-Méi (12-year-old curious girl, summer clothes) in Shots 1 and 4. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — DARLING DARLING (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A bumpy reddish-pink lychee with one section of shell dramatically peeled back to reveal glowing pearl-white flesh, fanning herself with a tiny leaf, sitting elegantly on a sunlit branch in a Nantou orchard. A shaft of afternoon sunlight catches her flesh, sparkling like a jewel. Theatrical, fancy storybook illustration with pink, white, and gold tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'Darling, DARLING,' Lìzhī announced, fanning herself with a leaf, 'do you SEE this skin? Bumpy. Rough. Positively prehistoric. But PEEL back the layers, darling, and you'll find the most exquisite, translucent, pearl-white flesh you have ever laid eyes on.' She demonstrated. 'Perfection. I am literally perfection in a bumpy jacket.'"

---

## Shot 2 — NANTOU ORCHARD (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wide establishing shot of a Nantou lychee orchard in central Taiwan — green mountains in the distance, a hundred-year-old gnarled lychee tree heavy with rosy-pink fruit clusters. Warm afternoon sun streams through. Lush, idyllic storybook illustration with deep green and pink tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Lìzhī lived in the finest lychee orchard in Nántóu county, in the green mountains of central Taiwan. Her tree was a hundred years old, gnarled and majestic, and it produced lychees so sweet that poets had written about them. (Admittedly, one of the poets was Lìzhī herself.)"

---

## Shot 3 — THE FORBIDDEN TREE (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`At the back of the orchard, behind a rusty old fence, stands a wild gnarled tree with darker bark almost black, hung with small dark brown lopsided lychee clusters. Mr. Huáng the orchard owner stands nearby shaking his head. The bumpy pink lychee Lìzhī observes from a safe distance. Mysterious, foreboding storybook illustration with deep greens and dark reds.`

**Narration:**
> "But there was one tree nobody touched. It stood at the very back, behind a rusty fence, its branches wild, its lychees dark and small and strange-looking. Mr. Huáng called it the 'old tree.' 'Forbidden,' he said. 'Bad fruit. Ugly. Not for selling.' 'Poor things,' Lìzhī said. 'Not everyone can be gorgeous.'"

---

## Shot 4 — XIǍO-MÉI ARRIVES (0:50–1:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A curious 12-year-old girl in summer clothes walking through the Nantou orchard with intense investigative attention, asking her grandfather Mr. Huáng a question. Sunny, warm afternoon light. Inquisitive storybook illustration with green orchard and warm gold.`

**Narration:**
> "Everything changed when a girl named Xiǎo-Méi came to the orchard. Twelve years old, visiting her grandfather Mr. Huáng for the summer, with the kind of curiosity that made adults nervous. 'Grandpa, why is that tree behind a fence?' 'Bad fruit.' 'Have you ever tasted them?' 'No need. You can TELL by looking.'"

---

## Shot 5 — CLIMBING THE FENCE (1:05–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A 12-year-old girl in summer clothes climbing over the rusty fence into the forbidden section of the orchard, while a fancy bumpy pink lychee on a nearby branch calls out a worried "be careful!" The dark old tree looms ahead. Adventurous storybook illustration with golden afternoon light.`

**Narration:**
> "On the fourth day, she climbed the fence. 'Xiǎoxīn!' Lìzhī called from her tree — 'be careful!' — not because she was worried, but because climbing fences was undignified and dignity was important. Xiǎo-Méi ignored the fancy lychee and walked through the tall grass to the old tree."

---

## Shot 6 — PEELING THE UGLY ONE (1:25–1:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Close-up of a girl's hands carefully peeling back the dark reddish-brown rough shell of a small lychee — and revealing astonishing DEEP PINK translucent flesh inside, glowing like stained glass in the sunlight. A symphony of fragrance suggested by floating small pink-flower swirls. Magical, breathtaking storybook illustration with golden light and shocking deep pink.`

**Narration:**
> "She peeled it. And that's when Lìzhī fell off her branch — emotionally, because what Xiǎo-Méi revealed was the most extraordinary thing Lìzhī had ever seen. The flesh inside wasn't white. It was PINK. A deep, blushing, rosy pink, translucent as stained glass, with a fragrance like flowers and honey and summer rain."

---

## Shot 7 — THE FIRST BITE (1:50–2:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A 12-year-old girl biting into a peeled deep-pink heritage lychee, her eyes going so wide they almost leave her face. An expression of pure transcendent shock. Sunlit orchard background. Energetic, joyful storybook illustration with gold light and shocking pink.`

**Narration:**
> "Xiǎo-Méi bit into it. Her eyes went so wide they almost left her face. 'Ōuyīxī!' she gasped. But the word felt pathetically inadequate. This wasn't yummy. This was TRANSCENDENT. Sweeter, deeper, with floral notes of rose and jasmine. 'GRANDPA! GRANDPA, COME HERE!'"

---

## Shot 8 — GRANDFATHER REALIZES (2:05–2:20)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Mr. Huáng standing under the old gnarled tree with new eyes, sitting down on the grass, looking at the small dark fruit and his granddaughter with the dawning expression of someone who has been wrong for a very long time. Profound, moving storybook illustration with warm afternoon light.`

**Narration:**
> "'Hǎochī,' he whispered — the way voices sound when someone realizes they've been wrong for a very long time. 'My grandfather planted this tree. A heritage variety. They stopped growing them commercially because they're too small. Too dark. They don't LOOK like what customers expect. I just looked at the fruit and saw something ugly, and I assumed.' 'You assumed wrong.'"

---

## Shot 9 — LÌZHĪ'S CRISIS (2:20–2:35)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A bumpy pink lychee on her branch in the pretty part of the orchard, going through a quiet existential crisis. Her tiny leaf fan drooping. Her dramatic posture wilted. The sunset light shifts the orchard into golden-pink. Tender, philosophical storybook illustration with pink, gold, and contemplative shadow.`

**Narration:**
> "Back on her branch, Lìzhī was having an existential crisis. She had spent her entire life believing that beauty was the outside. The shell. The presentation. And here was a fruit with the worst shell in the orchard and the most magnificent inside. 'Darling. DARLING. Have I been... wrong?' 'Peel back the layers,' she murmured, her own catchphrase turned back on herself like a mirror."

---

## Shot 10 — APOLOGY UNDER THE OLD TREE (2:35–2:55)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A bumpy pink lychee climbing down from her branch, crossing the orchard, squeezing through the rusty fence, and positioning herself on a branch of the dark old tree beside the small dark heritage lychees. A gentle nudge from a small dark lychee, smelling of roses. Tender, humble storybook illustration with golden hour light.`

**Narration:**
> "She climbed down from her beautiful tree, crossed the orchard, and squeezed through the fence. 'Darling. I owe you an apology. I judged you by your shell. And I, of all fruit, should have known better. My ENTIRE catchphrase is peel back the layers. Bù hǎo yìsi.' A small dark lychee nudged her gently. It smelled like roses."

---

## Shot 11 — TWO BASKETS AT THE STAND (2:55–3:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A roadside fruit stand with two baskets of lychees side by side — one of pretty rosy-pink shelled lychees, one of small dark heritage lychees showing pink flesh. A 12-year-old girl smiling between them. Customers peeling and tasting both. Warm, joyful storybook illustration with golden afternoon light and pink-and-green tones.`

**Narration:**
> "When the harvest came, Xiǎo-Méi picked from both trees. 'Which one should I buy?' 'Both,' said Xiǎo-Méi. 'One teaches you about beauty on the outside. The other teaches you about beauty on the inside. And together, darling, they teach you about beauty, period.' Lìzhī, sitting in the premium heritage basket beside her new friends, had never felt more beautiful."

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:15–3:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A peaceful Nantou orchard at deep dusk, both trees side by side now, their fruit catching the last gold light. A peeled lychee with deep pink flesh glowing softly on a branch. Sleepy, deeply tender storybook illustration with twilight pink, gold, and deep mountain green.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, darling dreamer, may you have the courage to peel back the layers — of the world, of others, and of yourself. May you never judge a fruit by its shell. The most extraordinary things are often hidden in the plainest packages. Goodnight, beautiful one. Goodnight."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Lìzhī = bumpy reddish-pink shell, often with one section peeled back to show pearl-white flesh, tiny leaf fan, dramatic theatrical posture. Heritage lychees = small, dark reddish-brown shells, surprising deep pink flesh inside (the visual reveal in Shot 6 should be SHOCKING). Xiǎo-Méi = 12 years old, summer clothes, bright curious eyes. Mr. Huáng = elderly grandfather, weathered face, gradually softening.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, Nantou orchard palette of pink, gold, and deep green" in every prompt. Lighting: bright afternoon (Shots 1-7), realization quietness (8-9), golden hour (10-11), twilight (12).
- **Audio:** Lìzhī's voice theatrical, dramatic, "darling"-heavy, then humbled and sincere by Shot 10. Xiǎo-Méi's voice young, curious, fearless. Mr. Huáng's voice gruff, then softened. Floral fragrance suggested through music.
- **Music:** Light playful strings for Lìzhī's vanity. Sense of discovery building under Shots 5-7. Tender warm strings under Shots 8-10. Triumphant warm reprise for Shot 11. Lullaby for goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Slow dissolves throughout. Hold dramatic close-ups: Shot 6 (the peel reveal) and Shot 7 (the first bite).
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 6 (the pink reveal) and Shot 10 (apology) deserve longer holds for emotional payoff.
