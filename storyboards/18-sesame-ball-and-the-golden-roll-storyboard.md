# Storyboard: Story 18 — Sesame Ball and the Golden Roll

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, golden-amber and deep lake-green palette, misty mountain atmosphere, soft edges, adventurous warmth

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Zhīma (perfectly round golden-fried ball covered in white sesame seeds, bold determined eyes, tiny strong legs, a slight crack revealing red bean paste inside) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots — his sesame coating should stay consistent even as water/mist affects his crunch.

---

## Shot 1 — ROLL WITH IT (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A golden-fried sesame ball covered in white sesame seeds launching off a night market snack cart at high speed, mid-air, bold determined eyes wide open. Motion lines streak behind him. The glittering expanse of Sun Moon Lake visible in the background with misty mountains. Adventurous storybook illustration, golden and emerald green tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'Roll with it!' he shouted, launching himself off the snack cart and down the hill toward the glittering expanse of Sun Moon Lake — the most beautiful lake in all of Taiwan and, as of three seconds ago, Zhīma's next conquest."

---

## Shot 2 — SUN MOON LAKE (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wide aerial view of Sun Moon Lake in Nantou County — emerald-green and midnight-blue waters, surrounded by misty mountain peaks and aboriginal villages, small fishing boats, a pier with a few tourists. Serene and ancient atmosphere. Peaceful storybook illustration with soft greens, blues, and misty silvers.`

**Narration:**
> "Sun Moon Lake sat in the mountains of Nantou County, its waters shifting between emerald green and midnight blue. The eastern half was shaped like a sun and the western half like a crescent moon. It was serene. Peaceful. Contemplative. Zhīma was none of these things."

---

## Shot 3 — THE OLD WOMAN (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A gentle, wise old woman in traditional Taiwanese clothing standing on a wooden pier, feeding fish from a paper bag. At her feet, a slightly dusty golden sesame ball has come to rest after a chaotic bounce. The old woman looks down with a knowing smile. Warm afternoon light, storybook illustration with soft gold and lake blue.`

**Narration:**
> "He bounced off a railing, ricocheted off a trash can, and came to rest at the feet of a very old woman feeding fish from the pier. 'You're in a hurry.' 'I'm ALWAYS in a hurry! Life is short! Sesame balls are crunchy! Crunch waits for no one!' 'Where are you going?'"

---

## Shot 4 — THE LEAP (0:50–1:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A golden sesame ball leaping off the end of a wooden pier over the green waters of Sun Moon Lake, arms outstretched confidently, sesame seeds catching the sunlight. Below, the water reflects his golden body. Dramatic adventurous storybook illustration with golden hour light and deep green water.`

**Narration:**
> "Finding the cave required crossing to the north side. He chose option three. 'Roll with it!' he shouted, and launched himself off the pier."

---

## Shot 5 — SINKING (1:05–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`An underwater view of a golden sesame ball sinking slowly into the green-dark depths of Sun Moon Lake, bubbles rising around him, startled fish swimming past, shafts of sunlight piercing the water from above. Ancient log resting on the lakebed below. Dreamy, slightly dramatic storybook illustration with green-gold underwater light.`

**Narration:**
> "He hit the water and immediately sank. Because sesame balls, as it turns out, are not boats. 'GLUB,' said Zhīma. He sank past startled fish, past waving water plants, past an ancient log that looked like it had opinions about impulsive sesame balls."

---

## Shot 6 — HEART WARMS (1:25–1:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Close-up underwater view of a golden sesame ball, a warm golden-red glow visible through a small crack revealing the sweet red bean paste heart inside. Determined eyes, tiny legs kicking, sesame seeds trapping air bubbles around him like tiny flotation devices. Magical storybook illustration with gold-amber inner glow against cool water blue.`

**Narration:**
> "But then something happened. The sweet bean paste inside Zhīma began to warm — a stubborn, sticky, golden warmth that refused to go cold. It was his heart. And it was saying: you're not done yet."

---

## Shot 7 — DOGGY-PADDLING ACROSS (1:40–2:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A waterlogged golden sesame ball doggy-paddling absurdly but determinedly across the emerald-green expanse of Sun Moon Lake, splashing, mountains in the background. A fisherman in a small wooden boat watches in amazement, mouth open. Comedic but heroic storybook illustration with gold, green, and misty blue tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Stroke after stroke, splash after splash, he crossed the water. It was absurd. It was undignified. It was a golden ball doing a doggy paddle across a sacred lake. But he did it. 'Jiāyóu!' called a fisherman — 'keep going!'"

---

## Shot 8 — WATERFALL CAVE (2:00–2:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A hidden waterfall tucked into a mossy fold of the mountainside, tumbling water screening a dark cave entrance. Ferns and bamboo frame the scene. A waterlogged golden sesame ball stands at the edge of a clear pool below, looking up at the cave. Atmospheric storybook illustration with emerald greens, silver mist, and shafts of golden light.`

**Narration:**
> "The waterfall was tucked into a fold in the mountainside, screened by ferns and bamboo. Behind the curtain of water was the cave entrance — just wide enough for a slightly soggy sesame ball to roll through."

---

## Shot 9 — INSIDE THE CAVE (2:15–2:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Inside a warm volcanic cave, glowing minerals in the walls cast everything in soft golden light. A golden sesame ball rolls through a narrow twisting passage, his drying crust beginning to crunch again. Mysterious, warm storybook illustration with amber glow against dark rock.`

**Narration:**
> "The cave was surprisingly warm. The volcanic rocks radiated gentle heat, drying Zhīma's soaked exterior and restoring some of his precious crunch. Glowing minerals in the walls cast everything in a soft golden light."

---

## Shot 10 — THE EMPTY PEDESTAL (2:30–2:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A chamber deep inside the cave with a natural stone pedestal in the center — but the pedestal is empty. A golden sesame ball gazes up at an ancient inscription carved into the pedestal in weathered Chinese characters. Soft mineral light glowing from the walls, reverent and mysterious storybook illustration with amber and deep earth tones.`

**Narration:**
> "On the pedestal was just an empty stone with an inscription: '勇氣不在外面，在裡面' — 'Courage is not outside. It is inside.' Zhīma stared for a long time. Then he looked down at himself. 'The treasure was never in the cave. The treasure is the bean paste.'"

---

## Shot 11 — SUNSET ON THE LAKE (2:50–3:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Sun Moon Lake at golden sunset, the water a mirror of gold and pink, mountains reflected perfectly in the surface. An old woman sits on a rock feeding fish from a paper bag. A small golden sesame ball sits beside her, both looking at the sunset together. Peaceful, contemplative storybook illustration with gold, amber, and deep blue tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Zhīma rolled out and sat on the shore. The old woman was there. 'Did you find what you were looking for?' 'I found out where I'm going. Wherever my heart takes me. But this time, I'm going to let it lead.'"

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:10–3:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A small child at a lakeside night market biting into a fresh golden sesame ball, red bean paste visible inside, their face full of delight. Behind them, Sun Moon Lake at deep twilight, mountains silhouetted, the first stars appearing. Warm lanterns glow from the food cart. Sweet, sleepy storybook illustration fading into gold and deep blue.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, brave one, may you roll with courage AND with heart. May your outside be crunchy and your inside be sweet. The lake is calm tonight. The mountains are watching over you. Goodnight, sesame warrior. Goodnight."

---

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Zhīma = perfectly round, golden-fried, covered in white sesame seeds, crack revealing red bean paste inside, bold determined eyes, tiny strong legs. Old woman = gentle, knowing smile, traditional Taiwanese clothing. Attach both references consistently.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, golden amber and emerald lake tones" in every prompt. Wet-look Zhīma for Shots 5-7, drying/crunchy by Shots 9-12.
- **Audio:** Zhīma's voice bold and punchy, even underwater (muffled "GLUB" then determined kicks). Old woman's voice warm and slow. Narrator extra warm for the "treasure is the bean paste" revelation.
- **Music:** Adventurous percussion for the leap and swim, warm contemplative strings for the cave and sunset, soft lullaby for the goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Splash effect between Shots 4-5. Dreamy slow dissolve into the cave (Shots 8-9). Tranquil dissolves from Shot 11 onward.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Extend Shot 6 (heart warming) and Shot 10 (inscription) for emotional weight.
