# Storyboard: Story 21 — Boba and the Midnight Demon Parade

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, warm colors, soft edges, cozy lighting

> **Tip:** Use a reference image for Boba and the demon kids on Shot 1, then attach it to all subsequent cards for character consistency. Add soft dissolve transitions between shots for bedtime story vibes.

---

## Shot 1 — THE SNEEZE (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny glowing red demon child with stubby crooked horns and enormous teary eyes hiding under a pineapple cake display at a busy night market. Warm neon lights, steam, lanterns. Whimsical storybook illustration style, soft warm colors.`

**Narration:**
> "The trouble started — as the best trouble always does — with a tiny sneeze. 'ACHOO!' It came from behind a stack of pineapple cakes at the Keelung night market..."

---

## Shot 2 — BOBA MEETS THE DEMON KID (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny glossy black tapioca pearl with huge eyes staring face-to-face with an adorable round red demon toddler with crooked horns, both under a night market table. Warm lantern light from above. Storybook illustration, cute and cozy.`

**Narration:**
> "Two enormous, adorable, tear-filled eyes stared back at her — set in a round red face no bigger than a rice ball, topped with two stubby horns..."

---

## Shot 3 — MORE DEMONS APPEAR (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A dozen tiny colorful demon children tumbling out from behind night market stalls — red, blue, green, purple, each with tiny horns and rosy cheeks. Some tripping, some giggling. Keelung night market background, neon signs, steam. Whimsical storybook animation style.`

**Narration:**
> "And then — like popcorn popping — more of them appeared. From behind the grilled squid cart. From inside a stack of steamer baskets. One rolled out of a garbage can wearing a noodle like a scarf..."

---

## Shot 4 — DEMONS WANT BOBA TEA (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Tiny adorable demon children pressing their faces against the glass of a bubble tea shop, eyes huge with longing, breath fogging up the window. Colorful boba tea drinks visible inside. Night market setting, warm glow.`

**Narration:**
> "What they wanted — MORE than anything — was boba milk tea. They'd heard about it in the spirit world. Legends. Songs. One demon had a tattoo of a boba cup on his arm (he was four)."

---

## Shot 5 — EVERYONE RUNS AWAY (1:10–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Night market vendors and customers running away screaming while tiny adorable demon toddlers stand looking confused and sad, one holding out a coin, another waving hello. Comedy scene, storybook illustration, exaggerated expressions.`

**Narration:**
> "But every time they tried to order, the vendors screamed and ran. Which was unfair, honestly. These were the least scary demons in the history of demons."

---

## Shot 6 — BOBA BEFRIENDS THEM (1:25–1:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny black tapioca pearl sitting in a circle with twelve adorable demon children on the night market ground, like a kindergarten story time. Demons are giggling, one is picking its nose, one is poking another. Warm, cozy storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "'Okay,' said Boba. 'Here's the deal. I'm a tapioca pearl. You're demons. We're all weird. Let's get you some tea.' The demons cheered so loudly a nearby vendor fainted."

---

## Shot 7 — DEMONS VS. BOBA TEA (comedy montage) (1:45–2:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Montage of tiny demon children hilariously failing at drinking boba tea — one trying to eat the straw, one scared of ice cubes and hissing at them, one with an entire boba pearl stuck on its horn. Night market setting, comedic storybook style.`

**Narration:**
> "The demons had never actually HAD boba tea before. One tried to eat the straw. Another was terrified of the ice cubes. A third somehow got a tapioca pearl stuck on his horn and wore it like a hat for the rest of the night."

---

## Shot 8 — THE BIG CUP (2:10–2:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`An enormous, festival-sized cup of boba milk tea being prepared on a stage, towering over the crowd. Night market competition banner in Chinese. Dramatic lighting, steam rising, crowd gathered around. Epic storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "That's when Boba saw it — the competition cup. The BIGGEST cup of boba milk tea ever assembled at the Keelung Ghost Month Festival. But there was a problem: not enough pearls."

---

## Shot 9 — BOBA'S SPEECH (2:25–2:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny tapioca pearl standing heroically on the rim of a giant boba tea cup, backlit by lantern light, giving a speech to a crowd of demon children looking up with sparkling eyes. Epic and silly at the same time. Storybook illustration, dramatic warm lighting.`

**Narration:**
> "'I was BORN for this cup,' Boba said. 'Every tapioca pearl dreams of the perfect tea. This is my moment. This is my DESTINY. Let's BOUNCE!'"

---

## Shot 10 — THE LEAP (2:40–2:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A glossy black tapioca pearl doing a magnificent cannonball dive into a giant cup of creamy brown milk tea, creating a splash. Tiny demon children cheering with arms raised. Dramatic slow-motion feel, golden light, storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "She bounced — one glorious, magnificent bounce — off the rim and into the tea with a PERFECT splash. The demon kids went absolutely wild."

---

## Shot 11 — DEMONS SLURPING (2:50–3:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Twelve adorable demon toddlers sharing one enormous boba tea through multiple straws, cheeks puffed out, eyes closed in bliss, tiny horns wiggling with happiness. Night market lanterns above. Warm, joyful storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "They slurped. Oh, how they slurped. Twelve little demons, twelve fat straws, one enormous cup. Every pearl, every drop, every sweet creamy sip. And Boba, rising through the straw, felt absolutely glorious."

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:10–3:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Tiny demon children walking back into a glowing spirit portal in the mist, waving goodbye, holding empty boba cups, some already falling asleep on each other's shoulders. Keelung night market fading into soft moonlight. Dreamy, sleepy storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, little dreamer, may you share something sweet with someone unexpected. The best treats taste better together. Goodnight, little boba. Goodnight."

---

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Generate a reference image of Boba (glossy black pearl, huge eyes, tiny arms/legs) and the demon kids (round, colorful, stubby horns, rosy cheeks) in Shot 1. Attach as reference to all subsequent shots.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, warm colors" in every prompt.
- **Audio:** Record narration or use CapCut AI voice. Sync clip duration to narration length.
- **Music:** Soft, playful background — CapCut has royalty-free lullaby/children's music.
- **Transitions:** Soft dissolves between all shots. Slightly longer dissolve on final shot.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Adjust by splitting or combining cards as needed.
