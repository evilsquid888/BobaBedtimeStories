# Storyboard: Story 07 — Shaved Ice and the Summer Meltdown

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, neon night market reds and tropical hot summer warmth balanced by cool ice blues and rainbow toppings, soft edges, dramatic flair

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Bīng (towering pile of fluffy white shaved ice loaded with mango syrup, condensed milk, red bean, taro ice cream, three gummy bears, tiny sunglasses, paper umbrella) and Piper (sleek tall Emperor penguin, glittery blue snowboard, confident gleam) in Shots 1 and 5. Attach to all subsequent shots — Bīng's pile shrinks visibly across the story.

---

## Shot 1 — THE MELTDOWN (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A towering, magnificent mountain of shaved ice piled with mango syrup, red bean, condensed milk, taro ice cream, gummy bears, tiny sunglasses on top, and a paper umbrella — but rivers of melt are running down its sides. The shaved ice character is wailing dramatically. Comedic, vibrant storybook illustration with warm neon and cool melt-blue tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'THIS,' Bīng announced to absolutely everyone within fifty meters, 'is LITERALLY the WORST DAY of my ENTIRE EXISTENCE.' He was a mountain of cuòbīng — towering, glorious, gravity-defying — and he was melting."

---

## Shot 2 — RAOHE NIGHT MARKET (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wide establishing shot of Raohe Night Market in Taipei — a long covered corridor lit by fluorescent lights, decorated with red banners, packed with sweaty happy people. Steaming food stalls everywhere. Vibrant atmospheric storybook illustration with rich red, neon yellow, and warm market light.`

**Narration:**
> "It was August in Taipei. The air temperature was approximately the same as the inside of a dragon's mouth. Rè sǐ le. The Ráohé yèshì was packed — hundreds of sweaty, happy people shuffling between food stalls in the thick summer air."

---

## Shot 3 — TOPPINGS LANDSLIDE (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Close-up of a melting shaved ice mountain mid-disaster — taro ice cream sliding sideways like a tiny purple avalanche, condensed milk running in white rivers, red bean topping cascading down. Tiny sunglasses sliding off the peak. Dramatic, comedic storybook illustration with vivid melting colors.`

**Narration:**
> "First, the taro ice cream scoop slid sideways like a tiny purple avalanche. Then the condensed milk started running in rivers. The red bean topping began a slow, tragic descent. 'I'm DIMINISHING. I'm becoming LESS. This is a TRAGEDY of EPIC PROPORTIONS.'"

---

## Shot 4 — DRAMA QUEEN VS. PEPPER BUN (0:50–1:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A pepper bun vendor next to the shaved ice stall rolling his eyes at a wailing melting shaved ice mountain. The shaved ice catches its tiny sunglasses as they slide and jams them defiantly back on. Comedic interaction storybook illustration with warm market light.`

**Narration:**
> "The pepper bun vendor next door rolled his eyes. 'You do this every night.' 'Because it happens every night! You're BREAD. You just get STALE. That takes DAYS. I'm losing myself in MINUTES.' 'Drama queen.' 'Drama KING, thank you very much.'"

---

## Shot 5 — PIPER ARRIVES (1:05–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A sleek tall Emperor penguin with confident eyes sliding to a stop in front of a melting shaved ice stall on a glittery blue snowboard, weaving through the night market crowd with Olympic grace. She flips her board up and catches it under one flipper. Cool, dynamic storybook illustration with bright market neon.`

**Narration:**
> "She came sliding — literally sliding — down the night market corridor on a glittery blue snowboard. 'Slide into it!' she said. 'Whoa — you look like you're having a rough night, mountain man.' 'I'm Piper Paddlefoot. I go where the ice is. And you, my drippy friend, are definitely ice.'"

---

## Shot 6 — KIND OF BEAUTIFUL (1:25–1:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`An Emperor penguin perched on a stall counter looking thoughtfully at a melting shaved ice mountain with rivers of mango syrup and condensed milk creating swirling patterns. The penguin tilts her head. Contemplative, oddly beautiful storybook illustration with vivid melting colors against neon background.`

**Narration:**
> "Piper looked at the puddle forming. 'Huh. That's rough. But also... kind of beautiful?' 'BEAUTIFUL? I'm a PUDDLE!' 'Not yet. You're somewhere between a mountain and a puddle. That's the interesting part.'"

## Shot 7 — THE WATER CYCLE (1:45–2:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A magical thought-bubble illustration showing the water cycle: a melting puddle becoming a gutter stream becoming evaporating cloud becoming rain becoming snow becoming a glacier becoming shaved ice again. Dreamy, educational storybook illustration with blue, white, and warm sunset gold.`

**Narration:**
> "'And then you'll evaporate. And become a cloud. And rain down on a mountain. And someone will collect the water and freeze it. And shave it into ice. And there you'll be again. Brand new. Even BIGGER mountain.' 'You really think so?' 'Slide into it, Bīng. Don't fight the melt. RIDE it.'"

## Shot 8 — COMEBACK SHOUT (2:05–2:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A half-melted but proud shaved ice mountain straightening its tiny sunglasses, raising its arms to the night market crowd, paper umbrella standing tall. Toppings cascading colorfully. A family approaching with wide-eyed kids. Triumphant, comedic storybook illustration with bright neon market light.`

**Narration:**
> "'MORE IS MORE!' Bīng bellowed, and even though he was now only half the mountain he'd been, he was twice as bright. 'Lái lái lái! GET your shaved ice! The MOST DELICIOUS, most BEAUTIFUL, most WONDERFULLY TEMPORARY shaved ice in ALL of Raohe Night Market!'"

## Shot 9 — THE FAMILY EATS (2:25–2:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A Taiwanese family — mother, father, two excited kids — sharing a bowl of magnificent half-melted shaved ice at a night market plastic table. The kids are laughing, syrup on their chins. Warm, joyful storybook illustration with night market neon and bright topping colors.`

**Narration:**
> "The family ordered. The vendor built fresh mountains. But it was Bīng's bowl they photographed: the slightly melted, perfectly imperfect, gloriously messy original. 'Hǎochī!' the father said. 'Ōuyīxī!' the kids shouted. They ate it all. Every drip, every drop, every melting, swirling, beautiful mouthful."

## Shot 10 — LAST CRYSTAL (2:45–3:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`An empty shaved ice bowl with just a tiny pool of sweet colorful water at the bottom and a paper umbrella floating on top like a tiny boat. The last single ice crystal glints faintly. Bittersweet, peaceful storybook illustration with soft warm market light fading.`

**Narration:**
> "Bīng's bowl was empty by 9:47 PM. Just a pool of sweet, colorful water at the bottom, with a paper umbrella floating on top like a tiny boat. 'Same time tomorrow?' Bīng's voice was faint now. 'Wouldn't miss it. Slide into it, Bīng.' 'More is more, Piper.' The last ice crystal melted."

## Shot 11 — A NEW MORNING MOUNTAIN (3:00–3:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A fresh block of frozen ice on a vendor's shaving machine in early morning light, a brand-new mountain of shaved ice rising under colorful new toppings — even taller, brighter, more toppings than before. The paper umbrella stands tall on top. Hopeful, triumphant storybook illustration with morning gold light and bright toppings.`

**Narration:**
> "The next morning, the vendor filled the ice shaver with a fresh block of frozen water — water that had once been rain, that had once been a cloud, that had once been a puddle on a night market floor. A new mountain rose. Taller. Brighter. Even more toppings. Bīng was back."

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:15–3:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A peaceful Raohe Night Market at dawn, lanterns dimmed, the corridor quiet. A single fresh shaved ice mountain glowing softly in the morning light, paper umbrella catching the first sunbeam. Sleepy, hopeful storybook illustration with soft amber dawn and cool ice blues.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, magnificent one, may you melt into sleep the way Bīng melts into the summer night — not with fear, but with the sweet knowledge that tomorrow you'll rise again. Nothing beautiful lasts forever. That's what makes it beautiful. Goodnight, sweet mountain. Goodnight."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Bīng = towering pile of fluffy white shaved ice with colorful toppings (mango syrup, red bean, condensed milk, taro ice cream, three gummy bears), tiny sunglasses, paper umbrella. Pile size SHRINKS visibly from Shot 1 (full mountain) to Shot 10 (just a puddle), then resets full in Shot 11. Piper Paddlefoot = sleek tall Emperor penguin, glittery blue snowboard, confident gleam.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, neon night market warmth meets cool ice blues" in every prompt. Topping colors should pop dramatically against everything else.
- **Audio:** Bīng's voice OVER-the-top dramatic, EVERYTHING in caps at first, mellowing to triumphant warmth by Shot 8. Piper's voice cool, confident, kind. Night market ambience throughout.
- **Music:** Dramatic operatic strings for the meltdown. Cool synth/lo-fi when Piper appears. Upbeat carnival music for the comeback (Shot 8). Soft contemplative for the empty bowl. Hopeful sunrise theme for the new mountain.
- **Transitions:** Quick comedic cuts during the meltdown drama. Slow magical dissolve through the water cycle (Shot 7). Long fade across the empty bowl (Shot 10) into the morning rebirth (Shot 11).
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 7 (water cycle) and Shot 11 (rebirth) deserve longer holds for the metaphor to land.
