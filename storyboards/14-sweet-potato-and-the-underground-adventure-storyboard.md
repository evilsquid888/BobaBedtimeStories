# Storyboard: Story 14 — Sweet Potato and the Underground Adventure

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, Beitou volcanic palette — deep earth brown, golden-orange flesh, sulfur-mist white, mineral cave colors (yellow, blue, crimson) — soft edges, warm hidden-treasure glow

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Dìguā (lumpy brown dirt-covered sweet potato with visible golden-orange cross-section, tiny hardhat, sturdy stance, street-smart expression) and Timmy (~11-year-old American boy, sandy hair, muddy adventure clothes, hand-drawn map) in Shots 1 and 4. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — UNDERGROUND HOMEBOY (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A lumpy brown dirt-crusted sweet potato with a tiny hardhat tapping his rough exterior, a visible glimpse of his golden-orange flesh inside revealed through a small crack. Standing beneath a Taiwanese sweet potato cart in Beitou. Rugged, street-smart storybook illustration with deep earth brown and warm gold tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Dìguā didn't look like much, and he'd be the first to tell you so. 'I'm golden on the inside,' he said, tapping his rough, dirt-crusted skin. 'But you gotta dig to find it. That's the whole point.' Taiwan's humblest hero. Lumpy, brown, earth-covered, and tasted like caramel when you roasted it."

## Shot 2 — BEITOU HOT SPRINGS (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wide establishing shot of Beitou hot springs district in northern Taipei — beautiful Japanese-era buildings, steaming streams, sulfuric mist drifting through. A converted oil-drum sweet potato cart with charcoal glow. Atmospheric storybook illustration with steam-mist white, warm wood, and golden lantern light.`

**Narration:**
> "His cart was parked near the Beitou Hot Springs Museum — a beautiful Japanese-era building surrounded by steaming streams. Uncle Cài had been roasting sweet potatoes in a converted oil drum for twenty-eight years. The drum glowed orange with charcoal heat."

## Shot 4 — UNDERGROUND KINGDOM MAP (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A boy spreading a muddy hand-drawn map on the ground beside a sweet potato cart. The map shows a network of tunnels in distinctive swirly Uncle Piggle Wiggle handwriting. A lumpy brown sweet potato with a hardhat pokes his head out of the cart's pile to study it. Curious, adventurous storybook illustration with weathered map and warm brown tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'I'm Timmy! I'm looking for the Underground Kingdom!' Dìguā poked his head out. 'Underground Kingdom, huh?' Timmy stared at the talking sweet potato, processed it for two seconds, then shrugged. After Uncle Piggle Wiggle, talking vegetables barely registered. 'Uncle Piggle Wiggle gave me the map. And a bottle of Courage Nectar, but I drank it on the plane.'"

## Shot 5 — DOWN THE CRACK (1:10–1:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Behind the Beitou Hot Springs Museum, a boy squeezes sideways into a narrow crack in volcanic rock, holding a sweet potato in his hand like a tiny flashlight. Steam hisses from the rock. Atmospheric, slightly mysterious storybook illustration with deep earth brown and steam-mist.`

**Narration:**

## Shot 6 — VOLCANIC TUNNEL (1:30–1:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Inside a warm volcanic tunnel — rocks radiating heat, sulfuric steam hissing from cracks in the walls creating ghostly wisps that curl around their feet. A boy with the sweet potato exploring deeper. Atmospheric, adventurous storybook illustration with deep brown rock and golden-amber glow.`

**Narration:**
> "The tunnel was warm. Volcanic warm. Steam hissed from cracks in the walls. 'This is AWESOME. It's like a real-life dungeon level.' 'It's not a game, kid.' 'I know! That's what makes it awesome! In games you can restart. This is real. No save points.'"

## Shot 7 — DIRT SENSE NAVIGATION (1:50–2:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A sweet potato with a hardhat at a tunnel branch, head tilted as if sensing the earth, golden-orange glow visible through the small crack in his exterior. The boy beside him watches in admiration. Mysterious, mystical storybook illustration with deep earth brown and amber glow.`

**Narration:**
> "'Left here. I can feel it. The earth is softer on the left. That means water. Water means caves.' 'How do you KNOW that?' 'Kid, I'm a sweet potato. I know dirt the way fish know water. This is my element.'"

## Shot 8 — THE CAVERN (2:05–2:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`An enormous cathedral-like underground volcanic cavern — ceiling lost in darkness, walls streaked with vivid mineral deposits in yellow, electric blue, deep crimson red. Hot spring pools bubble in different temperatures and colors. Steam rises and mixes. A boy and a sweet potato gasp in awe. Magnificent, magical storybook illustration with brilliant mineral colors and warm steam.`

**Narration:**
> "The left tunnel opened into a cavern that made them both gasp. Enormous — a cathedral of volcanic rock, walls streaked with mineral deposits in colors that shouldn't exist underground: vivid yellows, electric blues, deep crimson reds. 'Tài bàng le,' Timmy whispered. 'I've been under the ground my whole life,' said Dìguā. 'And I never knew THIS was down here.'"

## Shot 9 — THE STONE TABLET (2:30–2:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A boy and a sweet potato kneeling beside an ancient stone tablet half-buried in volcanic soil at the back of a cavern, brushing off dirt to reveal carved Classical Chinese characters. Soft mineral light from the cave walls. Reverent, mysterious storybook illustration with amber tablet glow and deep cave shadows.`

**Narration:**

The earth's kitchen — where the planet cooked its treasures. The greatest treasure is not gold or jade. The greatest treasure is the food that grows in humble soil, nourished by hidden fire. To find it, dig.'"

## Shot 10 — THE TREASURE IS SWEET POTATOES (2:50–3:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A boy sitting on a rock in the magnificent cavern, looking thoughtfully at a brown sweet potato beside him with new respect. The mineral lights bathe them both in warm color. Tender, philosophical storybook illustration with rainbow mineral cave glow.`

**Narration:**
> "'It's talking about sweet potatoes. The treasure is SWEET POTATOES. Taiwan's soil, heated by volcanic energy, enriched by mineral water — it grows the best sweet potatoes in the world. The treasure isn't a golden idol. It's the food that feeds millions of people, grown in dirt nobody looks twice at, powered by underground fire nobody sees.' 'I'm golden on the inside,' Dìguā repeated. And this time the catchphrase sounded like the truest thing anyone had ever said."

**Narration:**

His eyes closed. The flavor was volcanic heat and patient earth and hidden treasure transformed by fire into something golden. 'Hǎochī.'"

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Dìguā = lumpy brown dirt-covered exterior with visible golden-orange cross-section, tiny hardhat, sturdy stance, street-smart face. Always shows a glimpse of the gold inside. Timmy = ~11-year-old American boy, sandy hair, muddy clothes, wide enthusiastic eyes. Uncle Cài = elderly Taiwanese vendor, kind, charcoal-smudged hands.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, Beitou volcanic palette — earth brown, golden orange, sulfur mist, vivid mineral cave colors" in every prompt. Lighting deepens dramatically as they descend (Shots 5-8); brilliant mineral colors in the cavern (Shots 8-10); warm earth-glow back at the cart (11-12).
- **Audio:** Dìguā's voice down-to-earth, practical, streetwise but kind. Timmy's voice young, eager, gaming-language slipping in. Uncle Cài's voice gentle and grandfatherly. Tunnel echoes and steam hisses for Shots 5-9.
- **Music:** Adventurous percussion as they descend. Awe-struck swelling strings at Shot 8 (the cavern reveal). Warm philosophical strings at Shot 10. Tender warmth for the sweet potato eating moment. Lullaby for goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Camera-shake/dust transition into the cave (Shot 5). Slow magical reveal into Shot 8 (the cavern). Long contemplative dissolve into Shots 10 and 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 8 (cavern reveal) and Shot 11 (golden inside revealed) deserve longer holds for visual and emotional payoff.
