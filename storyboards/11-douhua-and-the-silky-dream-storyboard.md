# Storyboard: Story 11 — Douhua and the Silky Dream

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, Dadaocheng historic warmth — aged brick brown, ginger gold, ceramic white, soft window light — soft edges, contemplative quiet beauty

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Dòuhuā (smooth creamy white pudding cube, serene half-closed eyes, gentle smile, floats in a shallow ceramic bowl of golden ginger syrup, moves like tai chi) and Master Zhāng (73-year-old man, quiet, careful hands, traditional shop apron) in Shots 1 and 3. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — TAI CHI ON THE COUNTER (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A smooth creamy white tofu pudding cube with serene half-closed eyes practicing tai chi on the wooden counter of an ancient soy milk shop in Dadaocheng. Her movements are silky-smooth. A fly has landed nearby and fallen asleep watching. Cozy traditional shop interior, peaceful storybook illustration with cream, gold, and aged wood tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Dòuhuā moved the way water moves — slowly, gently. 'Be soft. Be strong,' she murmured, practicing tai chi on the wooden counter. A fly that had been buzzing landed on the counter to watch, forgot what it was doing, and fell asleep."

## Shot 3 — MASTER ZHĀNG AT WORK (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A 73-year-old man with quiet careful hands working soybeans into pudding inside a tiny traditional Taiwanese tofu shop. Steam rises from soy milk pots. A creamy white tofu pudding cube watches from a counter with calm respect. Warm shop interior, intimate storybook illustration with cream, gold, and aged brown.`

**Narration:**
> "The shop was Zhāng Family Tofu, making douhua the same way since 1952. Master Zhāng was seventy-three, got up at 3 AM every morning, and could turn soybeans into pudding with his eyes closed. Dòuhuā admired him. He was quiet, like her. He understood that gentleness is not weakness."

## Shot 5 — EMPTY SHOP (1:10–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`The interior of the quiet traditional tofu shop, completely empty. Master Zhāng standing in the doorway watching the line at the rival truck across the street. ## Shot 6 — STILL THINKING (1:25–1:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A creamy white tofu pudding cube sitting perfectly still on a wooden counter in slanting afternoon light, deep in meditation. Her serene half-closed eyes fully closed now. Soft, contemplative storybook illustration with golden afternoon light and cream-and-amber tones.`

**Narration:**
> "She could have gotten angry. Instead, she sat very still and thought. This was Dòuhuā's superpower. Where others reacted, she reflected. 'Water does not fight the rock. It flows around it. And eventually, the rock is smooth.'"

## Shot 7 — THE QUIET PLAN (1:40–2:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A creamy tofu pudding cube speaking softly to a quiet 73-year-old man at the counter. Their conversation is gentle, considered. Outside the window, the loud food truck still blasts. Tender, intimate storybook illustration with warm shop light and cool neon outside.`

## Shot 9 — THE LONG BREATH (2:20–2:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A woman sitting at the small outdoor table, her eyes closed, taking a slow long deep breath after her first bite of douhua. A half-eaten MEGA BOBA EXPLOSION abandoned beside her. Master Zhāng smiles gently from the shop doorway. Tender storybook illustration with warm dusk light and serene posture.`

**Narration:**
> "'Can I just have something simple?' Master Zhāng smiled. He ladled douhua into a bowl, added warm sweet ginger syrup and a spoonful of peanuts. The woman took a bite. She closed her eyes. She let out a breath so long and so deep it was like she'd been holding it for years. 'Hǎochī,' she whispered."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Dòuhuā = smooth creamy white pudding cube, serene half-closed eyes, gentle smile, floats in shallow ceramic bowl of golden ginger syrup. Moves like tai chi. Master Zhāng = 73-year-old man, quiet, careful hands, traditional shop apron, weathered face. DJ Dessert = young man, designer sunglasses, neon vibe.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, Dadaocheng historic warmth" in every prompt. Strong VISUAL contrast in Shot 4 between neon truck and ancient brick — let it be jarring.
- **Audio:** Dòuhuā's voice soft-spoken, poetic, meditative. Master Zhāng's voice quiet and weathered. The food truck blares EDM music and obnoxious shouting in Shots 4-5, 10. Quiet ambience for the douhua table.
- **Music:** Soft traditional erhu and guzheng for the shop. Sharp EDM contrast for the truck. Resolve to silence and breath sounds at Shot 9. Soft moonlit tone for Shot 11. Lullaby for goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Slow ceremonial dissolves throughout. Hard cut between the EDM truck (Shot 4) and the meditative shop (Shot 5-6) for stylistic contrast.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 6 (meditation), Shot 9 (the long breath), and Shot 11 (Master Zhāng eating) deserve longer holds for emotional weight.
