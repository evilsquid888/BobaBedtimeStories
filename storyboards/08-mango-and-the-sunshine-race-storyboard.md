# Storyboard: Story 08 — Mango and the Sunshine Race

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, sunlit orchard palette — bright orange, golden yellow, deep tropical green — soft edges, dynamic warm light

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Mango (sleek bright orange-yellow mango with a rosy blush on one cheek, tiny athletic sweatband, dripping golden juice) and Barnaby Beaksworth (chinstrap penguin, orange pizza-shaped snowboard, snack bib, friendly round face) in Shots 1 and 4. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — RUNNING THROUGH THE ORCHARD (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A sleek bright orange-yellow mango with a tiny athletic sweatband zooming through a Pingtung mango orchard at dawn, leaving a trail of golden juice droplets on the red dirt path. Rows of mango trees heavy with fruit. Energetic, dynamic storybook illustration with deep orange and golden green.`

**Narration:**
> "'Catch me if you can — I'm in season!' Mango shouted, zooming through the mango orchards of Pingtung like a small, orange, extremely competitive missile. His tiny sweatband was soaked with juice, his rosy blush was bright, and he was leaving a trail of golden droplets like a sprinter leaving sweat on a track."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Mango = sleek bright orange-yellow, rosy blush on one cheek, tiny athletic sweatband (always sweat-soaked), drips golden juice. Barnaby Beaksworth = round chinstrap penguin, orange pizza-shaped snowboard, snack bib, gentle wise face. Always eating.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, sunlit orchard palette of orange, gold, and deep green" in every prompt. Light progression: dawn (Shots 1, 7) → bright midday (3, 8, 9) → golden afternoon (10) → warm evening (11) → sunset (12).
- **Audio:** Mango's voice fast-talking, sporty, competitive. Barnaby's voice round, friendly, food-obsessed, gentle wisdom. Crowd cheers and orchard ambience for race scenes.
- **Music:** Energetic training-montage drums for Shots 1, 3, 9, 10. Soft warm strings for the philosophical sunrise (Shot 7). Festive market music for the tasting tour (Shot 8). Lullaby for goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Quick athletic cuts during running and racing scenes. Slow contemplative dissolve into Shot 7 (sunrise) and Shot 11 (the little girl eating). Long fade on Shot 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 7 (Pingtung sunrise) and Shot 10 (victory slide) deserve longer holds for the visual payoff.
