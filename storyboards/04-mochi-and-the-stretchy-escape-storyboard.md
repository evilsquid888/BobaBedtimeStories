# Storyboard: Story 04 — Mochi and the Stretchy Escape

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, MRT fluorescent palette warmed by friendship glow — soft white, warm pink, tropical sunlight — soft edges, cozy humor

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Mochi (soft pillowy white blob dusted with potato starch, rosy pink cheeks, ridiculously stretchy taffy arms, huge happy eyes) and Willa Wobble (Little Blue penguin, tiny purple snowboard, slate-blue feathers, enormous worried eyes) in Shot 3. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — GROUP HUG ATTACK (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A soft pillowy white mochi blob with rosy cheeks and stretchy taffy arms launching itself onto a startled businessman in a suit on a Taipei MRT subway car. The blob leaves powdery starch residue on his briefcase. He looks alarmed; she looks delighted. Comedic warm storybook illustration with orange seats and clean white MRT interior.`

**Narration:**
> "'GROUP HUG!!!' Mochi yelled, launching herself at a surprised commuter on the Taipei jiéyùn. The businessman suddenly found himself with a softball-sized white blob stuck to his briefcase. 'I just love your energy! Everyone needs a hug! STICK TOGETHER!'"

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Mochi = soft pillowy white blob, potato-starch dust, rosy pink cheeks, taffy-stretchy arms (showcase the stretch every chance), huge happy eyes. Willa Wobble = small Little Blue penguin (~10 inches tall), slate-blue feathers, enormous worried eyes, tiny purple snowboard always under flipper.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, MRT fluorescent palette warmed by character pink" in every prompt. Lighting shifts from cool MRT to warm sunlight when they exit (Shot 9).
- **Audio:** Mochi's voice exuberant, exclamation-heavy. Willa's voice quiet, gentle, growing braver. MRT ambience (announcements in multiple languages) in Shots 1-8.
- **Music:** Light bouncy strings for Mochi's chaos. Soft solo piano when Mochi slows down (Shot 6). Triumphant warm chords at Shot 9. Lullaby for the goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Stretchy "taffy" wipe between key shots (where possible in CapCut). Quick cuts during the wrong-turns montage (Shot 7). Slow dissolves into Shots 6, 11, 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 6 (slowing down) and Shot 11 (waving goodbye) deserve a longer hold for emotional weight.
