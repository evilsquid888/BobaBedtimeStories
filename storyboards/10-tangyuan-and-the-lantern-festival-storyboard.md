# Storyboard: Story 10 — Tangyuan and the Lantern Festival

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, lantern festival palette — warm orange paper-lantern glow, deep midnight blue sky, soft white rice and ginger gold — soft edges, magical warmth

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Tāng Yuán (perfectly round white glutinous rice ball, rosy cheeks, jolly warm expression, black sesame filling visible as a dark center) and the Chén family (5 members of varying ages plus 84-year-old Grandmother Chén) in Shots 1 and 6. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — IN THE THERMOS (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A perfectly round white glutinous rice ball with rosy cheeks bobbing happily inside a thermos of warm sweet ginger broth alongside eleven sibling tangyuan of various pastel colors (pink, white, light yellow). Cozy, intimate storybook illustration with golden ginger broth and steamy warmth.`

**Narration:**
> "'We're all in this soup together!' Tāng Yuán announced, bobbing in a pot of warm sweet ginger broth alongside eleven other tangyuan — his brothers, sisters, cousins, and one tangyuan of unclear relation who showed up at every family gathering and nobody had the heart to ask who invited her."

---

## Shot 2 — THE CAR RIDE (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`The interior of a Taiwanese family car driving toward Pingxi at dusk — Mama Chén, Bàba Chén, teenage daughter Yú-Xīn, twelve-year-old son Zhì-Míng, and 84-year-old Grandmother Chén in the back. A large thermos secured between them. Bickering, warm storybook illustration with twilight blue and warm interior light.`

**Narration:**
> "The Chén family was driving to Píngxī. 'The tangyuan is too sweet this year,' Grandmother Chén declared. 'Mama, you say that every year.' 'Because every year, it's too sweet. When I was a girl, tangyuan had CHARACTER.' Tāng Yuán chuckled inside the thermos. He loved the Chén family."

---

## Shot 3 — PINGXI ARRIVES (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`The tiny mountain town of Pingxi at dusk — every street lined with red lanterns, shops selling sky lanterns and firecrackers, families gathering. Excitement humming. Atmospheric, magical storybook illustration with deep red lantern glow and twilight mountain blue.`

**Narration:**
> "They arrived at dusk. Pingxi was transformed: every street lined with red lanterns, every shop selling sky lanterns. The air hummed with the excited chatter of a thousand families. 'Lái lái lái!' shouted the sky lantern vendors."

---

## Shot 4 — WRITING WISHES (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`The Chén family gathered around a large white sky lantern at a launch site, each holding a brush and writing wishes in bold Chinese calligraphy on the paper. Grandmother Chén in elegant old script, Bàba Chén bold strokes, Mama Chén elegant, Yú-Xīn covering hers, Zhì-Míng staring at his blank section. Warm dusk light. Intimate storybook illustration with white lantern paper and ink-black calligraphy.`

**Narration:**
> "'Everyone writes a wish.' Grandmother wrote 'Health for my family.' Bàba wrote 'A good year for the business.' Mama wrote 'Peace of mind.' Yú-Xīn covered hers and refused to show. Zhì-Míng stared at his blank section. His brush hovered. He didn't write."

---

## Shot 5 — TOGETHER FOREVER (1:10–1:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A twelve-year-old boy carefully writing 永遠在一起 (yǒngyuǎn zài yīqǐ) on a sky lantern in messy but heartfelt calligraphy. His family stands quietly behind him, Grandmother Chén's hand on his shoulder. Heartfelt, tender storybook illustration with warm dusk lantern light.`

**Narration:**
> "'I don't know what to wish for. Everything I can think of sounds dumb.' He stared at the ground. 'I wish... I wish we could do this forever. This. All of us. Together. I wish nothing would change.' Grandmother Chén cleared her throat. 'Write that. That is the best wish on this lantern.'"

---

## Shot 6 — THE COUNTDOWN (1:30–1:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Hundreds of families on a launch field at full dusk, each holding a glowing paper sky lantern aloft, candles lit inside making them warm orange. The Chén family gathered around their lantern, each holding an edge. Magical, breathless storybook illustration with deep blue sky and rivers of warm orange lantern light.`

**Narration:**
> "Across the launch field, hundreds of families held their lanterns aloft. Candles were lit. The lanterns began to glow — warm, orange, alive. 'Three!' the crowd shouted. The Chén family gathered around their lantern, each holding an edge. 'Two!' Grandmother squeezed Zhì-Míng's hand. 'One!' 'Fàng!'"

---

## Shot 7 — A THOUSAND LANTERNS RISE (1:45–2:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A thousand sky lanterns rising slowly into a black mountain sky above Pingxi, becoming a river of warm orange light flowing upward like fire flowing the wrong way. Faces in the crowd lit by the soft glow, hushed wonder. Breathtaking storybook illustration with warm orange lights against deep midnight blue.`

**Narration:**
> "A thousand lanterns rose. The lanterns rose higher, becoming a river of light flowing into the black mountain sky. Each one carried a wish: for health, for love, for happiness, for things too private and too precious to say out loud. And among them, in the messy calligraphy of a twelve-year-old boy: together forever."

---

## Shot 8 — TĀI BÀNG LE (2:10–2:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A close-up of 84-year-old Grandmother Chén, her face softly lit from below by the rising lanterns, eyes brimming with quiet tears, no criticism this time. Her hand still holding her grandson Zhì-Míng's hand. Tender, intimate storybook illustration with warm orange lantern glow on weathered loving features.`

**Narration:**
> "'Tài bàng le,' Grandmother Chén breathed — 'beautiful' — and for once, she had no criticism to add."

---

## Shot 9 — NIGHT MARKET TABLE (2:25–2:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`The Chén family seated around a plastic table at a Pingxi night market stall, each with a steaming bowl of tangyuan in golden ginger soup. Distant sky lanterns visible as tiny lights still in the dark sky above. Mama Chén ladling. Warm storybook illustration with golden bowl glow and twilight blue.`

**Narration:**
> "Afterward, the family sat at a plastic table. Mama Chén unscrewed the thermos and ladled out bowls of tangyuan in sweet ginger soup — one bowl for each family member, with three tangyuan per bowl. 'Chī bǎo le ma?' 'Wait!' said Grandmother Chén. 'We eat together. On the count of three.'"

---

## Shot 10 — TOGETHER ON THREE (2:45–3:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Five spoons dipping simultaneously into five steaming bowls of tangyuan soup at the count of three. Five sets of eyes closing in the same expression of warm sweet satisfaction. The black sesame filling of one tangyuan visible bursting open. Joyful, intimate storybook illustration with golden broth and warm steam.`

**Narration:**
> "'Yī... èr... sān!' Five spoons dipped at the same moment. Five bites. Five sets of eyes closing. Tāng Yuán felt Zhì-Míng's teeth press gently through his glutinous rice skin, and the black sesame filling burst free — warm, sweet, nutty, and rich. 'Ōuyīxī,' Zhì-Míng said softly, his eyes shining."

---

## Shot 11 — THE TASTE OF HOME (3:00–3:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A close-up tender shot of twelve-year-old Zhì-Míng eating tangyuan with his family, distant lanterns still glowing in the dark sky behind them through the night market stall opening. The whole family smiling and chewing. Magical, tender storybook illustration with golden steam and deep blue night sky.`

**Narration:**
> "Inside that bite — inside that moment of warm soup and sweet sesame and family gathered under a sky full of floating wishes — Tāng Yuán felt it. Togetherness. Not the kind that lasts forever — but the kind that MATTERS forever. The kind that becomes a memory so warm and sweet it stays with you for the rest of your life. The kind that tastes like home."

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:15–3:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A peaceful Pingxi mountain night with the last few sky lanterns drifting up into a starlit sky, becoming part of the constellation. A single steaming bowl of tangyuan glows on a windowsill. Sleepy, magical storybook illustration with deep midnight blue and warm orange points of light.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, beloved one, may you float in the warm soup of your family's love — whoever your family is, however they found you. The lanterns are rising. The wishes are flying. Close your eyes, round and warm as a tangyuan, and know that you are never, ever alone. Goodnight, little rice ball. Goodnight."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Tāng Yuán = perfectly round white glutinous rice ball, rosy cheeks, jolly warm expression, black sesame filling visible as a warm dark center. Tangyuan family = various pastel colors (pink with peanut, white with sesame, light yellow). Chén family = consistent across shots — Mama, Bàba, teenage Yú-Xīn, 12-year-old Zhì-Míng, 84-year-old Grandmother (kind weathered face).
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, lantern festival warmth — orange paper-lantern glow on deep midnight blue" in every prompt. Lighting builds from interior thermos (Shot 1) to dusk (3-5) to lantern-lit night (6-12).
- **Audio:** Each Chén family member should sound distinctive — Grandmother's voice opinionated and warm, Bàba steady, Mama gentle, Yú-Xīn teenage, Zhì-Míng quiet and earnest. Tāng Yuán's voice hearty and inclusive ("we" not "I"). Narrator extra warm for the home scenes.
- **Music:** Quiet folk strings during the car/family scenes. Magical building theme as lanterns rise (Shot 7). Tender warm strings for the family meal. Soft lullaby for the goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Slow magical dissolves throughout. Hold long on Shot 7 (lanterns rising), Shot 8 (Grandmother's tears), Shot 11 (taste of home), and Shot 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shots 6-7 (countdown and rising) and Shot 11 (taste of home) deserve longer holds for the emotional climax.
