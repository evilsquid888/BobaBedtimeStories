# Storyboard: Story 03 — Aiyu and the Mountain Spring

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, ancient cypress green and golden-amber palette, mountain mist atmosphere, soft edges, ethereal warm light

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Aiyu (wobbly pale golden-amber translucent jelly cube, tiny lemon-seed freckles, shy thoughtful eyes, faint inner glow) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots — her transparency should genuinely show what's behind her in every frame.

---

## Shot 1 — SEE-THROUGH IN A PUDDLE (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A wobbly translucent pale golden-amber jelly cube with tiny lemon-seed freckles staring at her own reflection in a small mountain puddle. Behind her, a small beetle is visible THROUGH her translucent body. She looks self-conscious. Soft morning light, mossy forest floor. Gentle storybook illustration with green and amber tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Aiyu was see-through, and she hated it. 'You can see right through me,' she sighed. 'Literally. There's a beetle behind me and you can count its legs through my stomach.'"

---

## Shot 2 — ALISHAN MAJESTY (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wide establishing shot of Alishan — ancient cypress trees taller than apartment buildings, clouds rolling slowly through deep valleys, sunlight in golden beams through the canopy. Magnificent, awe-inspiring storybook illustration with deep green, gold, and silver mist.`

**Narration:**
> "Aiyu lived on the slopes of Ālǐshān, the most famous mountain in all of Taiwan. The kind of place that made poets write poems and photographers forget to press the shutter button because they were too busy crying about the beauty."

## Shot 3 — THE HIKING GROUP ARRIVES (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A vintage narrow-gauge train pulling into Alishan trailhead, hikers spilling out in a riot of hiking boots, selfie sticks, and oversized water bottles. A guide named Mr. Chén holds a megaphone. Cheerful chaos, warm morning light, storybook illustration with greens and warm earth tones.`

**Narration:**
> "They came up the Alishan Forest Railway and spilled out at the trailhead. 'Lái lái lái!' Mr. Chén bellowed through his megaphone. 'Today we hike to the Sacred Spring! The water is so pure you can see to the bottom! So clear! So honest! Just like the famous Alishan aiyu jelly!'"

---

## Shot 4 — JIGGLING ALONG THE TRAIL (0:50–1:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A small translucent golden-amber jelly cube hopping from rock to rock along a misty mountain trail through a cathedral of moss-draped cypress trees. Sunlight filters through the canopy in golden beams. Whimsical, slightly comedic storybook illustration with deep green and gold.`

**Narration:**
> "Aiyu, curious, jiggled along behind them, hopping from rock to rock. The trail wound through a cathedral of cypress trees, their trunks wrapped in moss like green velvet coats. Sunlight filtered through the canopy in golden beams."

## Shot 5 — THE PHONE FALLS (1:05–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A twelve-year-old boy named Wèi leaning over the railing of a narrow wooden bridge, watching in horror as his phone bounces off a rock and disappears into a deep dark pool below. Cypress trees around. Dramatic, comedic storybook illustration with deep green, dark water, and sun-dappled wood.`

**Narration:**
> "A boy named Wèi leaned over the railing to take a photo and dropped his phone. 'NO!' He watched it bounce off a rock and disappear into a deep, dark pool. The locals called it the Liar's Pool, because the water was so dark you couldn't see the bottom."

---

## Shot 6 — DECISION AT THE FERN (1:25–1:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A small translucent golden-amber jelly cube peeking out from behind a fern, watching the boy's defeated face. Her own translucent body catches a beam of sunlight, glowing softly. Conflicted, brave, intimate storybook illustration with green and gold.`

**Narration:**
> "Aiyu, watching from behind a fern, felt her translucent heart squeeze. She could help. She KNEW she could help. But it meant doing the one thing she hated most: being seen through. 'Oh, for goodness' sake. Aiyu. Your name literally means love jade. Act like it.'"

---

## Shot 7 — INTO THE LIAR'S POOL (1:40–2:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Underwater cross-section view: a translucent golden-amber jelly cube sinking through dark tannin-stained water, glowing faintly like a tiny lantern, illuminating coins, a moldy hiking boot, and a fish skeleton on the pool floor. Magical, eerie storybook illustration with deep brown water and warm amber glow.`

**Narration:**
> "She jiggled out from behind the fern and plopped into the Liar's Pool. The dark water that hid everything from everyone? Aiyu could see through it. Not because the water was clear — because SHE was clear. Her translucent body let the faintest light pass through like a living lens."

**Visual Prompt:**

**Narration:**

## Shot 10 — THE COOLEST THING EVER (2:30–2:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wèi holding the small translucent jelly cube up to eye level, his fingers visible through her amber-tinted body. Aiyu jiggling shyly. Wèi's face breaking into the biggest grin. Tender, warm storybook illustration with golden afternoon light and forest green.`

**Narration:**
> "'I can see through things,' Aiyu said softly. 'That's kind of my whole deal. I'm see-through. What you see is what you get.' She expected disappointment. Instead, Wèi's face broke into the biggest grin she had ever seen. 'That's the coolest thing EVER.'"

---

**Narration:**

**Narration:**

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Aiyu = wobbly, pale golden-amber translucent cube, tiny lemon-seed freckles, faint moonlight glow. Her transparency MUST be visible in every shot — the world behind her should always show through her body. Wèi = 12 years old, kind face. Mr. Chén = guide with megaphone, enthusiastic.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, ancient cypress green and golden amber, mountain mist" in every prompt. Underwater shots (7-8) shift to dark brown-tannin tones with amber glow.
- **Audio:** Aiyu's voice quiet, thoughtful, growing more confident across the story. Wèi's voice young and earnest. Mr. Chén's voice loud and theatrical through megaphone.
- **Music:** Soft mountain flutes and gentle strings. Underwater shots (7-8) drop to a hushed, magical tone. Triumphant warmth for the surfacing moment.
- **Transitions:** Slow dissolves throughout. Underwater "ripple" or wave transition into Shot 7 and back out of Shot 9.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 7 (sinking) and Shot 11 (the cup at sunset) deserve longer holds for visual beauty.
