# Storyboard: Story 15 — Coconut Jelly and the Beach Party

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, tropical sunset palette — turquoise, coral, gold, lavender — soft edges, warm cozy lighting

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Yēguǒ (translucent white jelly cube with tropical shimmer, tiny flip-flops, shell necklace, chill half-smile) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots for character consistency. Use slow dissolves to match the "go with the flow" vibe.

---

## Shot 1 — FLOATING ON A WAVE (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A translucent white coconut jelly cube with a tropical shimmer lying on a small surfboard, floating on turquoise water at Kenting Beach. He wears a tiny shell necklace and has a peaceful half-smile. ## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Yēguǒ = translucent white cube with tropical shimmer, tiny flip-flops, shell necklace, chill half-smile. Always calm, half-lidded eyes. Attach his reference to every shot.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "tropical sunset palette, warm storybook illustration" in every prompt. Shift from afternoon brightness (Shots 1-5) to golden hour (6-8) to sunset gold/coral (9) to night (10-12).
- **Audio:** Narrator voice should be warm and bedtime-soft. Yēguǒ's lines delivered laid-back, surfer cadence.
- **Music:** Mellow ukulele or lo-fi tropical for Shots 1-7, build into beach-party drums for 8-10, return to soft tides for 11-12.
- **Transitions:** Slow, dreamy dissolves — no hard cuts. Slightly longer hold on Shot 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Stretch Shot 9 (sunset transformation) a beat longer for the visual payoff.
