# Storyboard: Story 16 — Egg Waffle and the Night Market Showdown

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, golden-brown and warm neon tones, steamy night market atmosphere, soft edges, cozy lighting

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Dàn (golden-brown egg-shaped waffle, crispy grid pattern, tiny bottle-cap top hat, huge enthusiastic eyes) and Finnegan (Rockhopper penguin with yellow-orange crest feathers, red fish-sticker snowboard, wild eyes) in Shots 2 and 6. Attach to all subsequent shots for consistency.

---

## Shot 1 — STEP RIGHT UP (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny golden-brown egg waffle cake the size of a golf ball, wearing a bottle-cap top hat, standing on a night market counter with arms flung wide in a "ta-da!" pose. Warm steam rising from a cast-iron griddle behind him. Fengjia Night Market neon glow, whimsical storybook illustration, golden-brown tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'STEP RIGHT UP, FOLKS! Step RIGHT up!' Dàn's voice could be heard three stalls down, which was impressive for a waffle cake the size of a golf ball."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Dàn = golden-brown egg waffle, crispy grid pattern, bottle-cap top hat, huge enthusiastic eyes, tiny arms. Finnegan = Rockhopper penguin, yellow-orange crest feathers, red fish-sticker snowboard, wild-eyed grin, manic energy. Attach both references to every shot.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, warm golden-brown and neon night market" in every prompt.
- **Audio:** Dàn's lines should have carnival-barker energy — punchy, loud, theatrical. Finnegan's lines delivered at breakneck speed with a grin.
- **Music:** Night-market bustle ambience underneath. Tempo dips low at Shots 5-6 (lonely counter), builds triumphantly through Shots 8-10.
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts during Finnegan's crash-in (Shot 6) and the performance montage (Shots 8-10). Slow dissolves into Shot 11 and the goodnight.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 6 and Shot 10 can run longer for comedic/emotional payoff.
