# Storyboard: Story 19 — Starfruit and the Five-Pointed Wish

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, twilight-to-starry-night palette — soft green-yellow, silver, midnight blue, warm amber — dreamy glow, ethereal edges

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Yángmáo (five-pointed star shape when sliced, pale green-yellow with translucent edges that catch light like stained glass, tiny star-shaped freckles, dreamy wide eyes, soft glow at the edges) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots — her glow should intensify subtly as the sky darkens.

---

## Shot 1 — EVERY POINT IS A WISH (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A translucent pale green-yellow starfruit with five points held up against a twilight sky, her edges glowing softly and catching the last light of day. Stars beginning to appear above her like tiny holes punched in a dark blanket. Ethereal, dreamy storybook illustration with lavender twilight and soft gold.`

**Narration:**
> "Yángmáo had five points, and each one was a wish. 'Every point is a wish,' she whispered, holding herself up to the twilight sky and watching the stars appear one by one above Yangmingshan, like someone was poking holes in a dark blanket to let the light through."

---

## Shot 2 — THE TRAIL BEGINS (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A translucent pale green-yellow five-pointed starfruit beginning to hike up a bamboo-lined trail on Yangmingshan at dusk, the trail winding from the suburban edge of Taipei up into wild bamboo forests. Golden suburban lights below, deep green bamboo ahead. Adventurous storybook illustration with lavender dusk and warm gold.`

**Narration:**
> "She began the climb at dusk. The trail up Yángmíngshān started in the suburbs of Taipei and quickly transformed into something wild. Bamboo forests closed in on both sides, their stalks creaking in the wind like a thousand tiny doors opening and closing."

---

## Shot 3 — POINT ONE: OCEAN ABOVE CLOUDS (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A small translucent starfruit standing on a wooden rest platform overlooking a valley filled with clouds below. Beyond the clouds, a thin silver line of the Pacific Ocean glints under the last sunset light. The starfruit glows gently, one of her five points catching the horizon. Breathtaking storybook illustration with cloud-white, silver, and peach sunset tones.`

**Narration:**
> "The clouds had settled below her. And beyond them, far in the distance, the Pacific Ocean glinted under the last light of the sun. 'The ocean from above the clouds,' Yángmáo breathed. 'Point One.'"

---

## Shot 4 — POINT TWO: RAIN MID-AIR (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A starfruit in a misty cloud forest with gnarled moss-draped trees and ferns, her mouth open to catch a raindrop that hangs magically in the air an inch from her face, caught mid-materialization. Tiny droplets float everywhere like glitter. Magical, otherworldly storybook illustration with silver mist and soft green glow.`

**Narration:**
> "A raindrop formed an inch from her face, hung in the air for a fraction of a second, and then — before gravity could claim it — landed on her tongue. 'Rain before it touches the ground. Point Two.'"

---

## Shot 5 — SULFUR VENTS AND SILVER GRASS (1:10–1:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A volcanic mountain landscape on Yangmingshan — sulfur vents hissing steam into the air, a meadow of silver grass with white plumes swaying in the evening breeze, a stone monument worn smooth on the trail. A small translucent starfruit rolls past, glowing faintly. Atmospheric, slightly surreal storybook illustration with silver, steam-white, and twilight purple tones.`

**Narration:**
> "She passed sulfur vents where volcanic steam hissed out like the earth was breathing. She passed a meadow of silver grass, its white plumes swaying like a ghost army doing synchronized yoga. She touched a stone monument worn smooth by a million hands."

---

## Shot 6 — THE SAD MAN ON THE BENCH (1:30–1:55)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A middle-aged man in a wrinkled suit with a loosened tie sits on a bench beside the mountain trail, staring at his hands with exhausted sadness. A translucent starfruit sits on the armrest beside him, gently glowing. The man begins to smile for the first time. Warm, tender storybook illustration with soft amber and twilight purple.`

**Narration:**
> "'Did you know,' said Yángmáo, 'that when you slice a starfruit, it becomes a star? I spend my whole life looking like a weird waxy lump, and then someone slices me and suddenly — STAR. We're all stars, waiting to be discovered. Some of us are just... not sliced yet.' The man laughed. A rusty, good laugh. 'Thank you. I needed that.'"

---

## Shot 7 — THE CRESCENT MOON (1:55–2:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A thin crescent moon rising over the silhouette of Taipei far below, glowing like a curved lantern. A small translucent starfruit sits on a rock looking up at it, her five points catching the moonlight. City lights twinkling like a fallen galaxy below. Peaceful, contemplative storybook illustration with deep midnight blue, silver moonlight, and warm gold city glow.`

**Narration:**
> "'Why do you change shape?' she asked the moon. The moon didn't answer. But watching the silver crescent climb the sky, she understood. The moon didn't actually change. It was always a sphere — always whole. The light just hits differently. 'Like me.'"

---

## Shot 8 — THE SUMMIT UNDER THE MILKY WAY (2:15–2:35)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A starfruit sitting on the highest rock at the summit of Yangmingshan, surrounded by silver grass and sulfur mist, looking up at a breathtaking Milky Way stretching across a black velvet sky. The city of Taipei glows far below. Epic, magical storybook illustration with deep blacks, powdery silver stars, and soft green-yellow character glow.`

**Narration:**
> "She sat on the highest rock she could find, surrounded by silver grass and sulfur mist and a sky so full of stars it looked like someone had spilled a bag of sugar across black velvet. The city of Taipei glowed below like a galaxy that had fallen to earth."

---

## Shot 9 — THE SECRET WISH (2:35–2:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`An extreme close-up of a glowing translucent starfruit looking up at the star-filled sky, her five-pointed edges shimmering with emotion, a single tear of dew trembling on one point. The Milky Way reflected in her translucent body. Intimate, emotional storybook illustration with silver, soft green-yellow glow, and stardust.`

**Narration:**
> "'I wish,' she said, very softly, 'to know that my dreams matter.' Not to have her dreams come true. Just to know they MATTERED. That dreaming wasn't foolish."

---

## Shot 10 — THE FIVE STARS ALIGN (2:50–3:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A magical sky moment — five bright stars aligned in a perfect five-pointed star pattern directly overhead, glowing brighter than the others, stardust trailing between them. A tiny translucent starfruit below glows brilliantly, bathed in starlight, her edges shining like stained glass. Awe-struck, magical storybook illustration with deep midnight blue and brilliant silver-gold stars.`

**Narration:**
> "Five stars in the sky aligned in a pattern that looked, from exactly this angle, like a five-pointed star. It lasted three seconds. Maybe four. But for those three seconds, the sky had answered her. Your dreams matter. You are a star."

---

## Shot 11 — STARFRUIT JUICE STAND (3:05–3:20)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A cozy night market juice stand at the base of the mountain, cups of fresh starfruit juice lined up on the counter, each containing perfect five-pointed starfruit slices floating in golden liquid, glowing under warm streetlights. A customer sipping happily. A starry mountain sky visible above the market awning. Sweet, warm storybook illustration with golden and midnight blue tones.`

**Narration:**
> "A night market vendor was selling fresh starfruit juice — sliced starfruit blended with honey and ice. 'Ōuyīxī!' a customer said, sipping — 'yummy!' And Yángmáo felt pride. Because every slice was a star. Every star was a wish. And every wish — spoken or unspoken — mattered."

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:20–3:35)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A dreamy night sky full of stars fading from view as dawn approaches, a single glowing five-pointed starfruit silhouette drifting peacefully among the stars. Soft, ethereal, sleepy. Storybook illustration fading into deep blue, silver, and soft gold.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, starry dreamer, may your five points shine bright. Your dreams are not foolish. Your wishes are not small. You are a star — maybe not sliced yet, maybe not seen yet, but always whole, always shining. Every point is a wish. And every wish matters. Goodnight, little star. Goodnight."

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Yángmáo = translucent pale green-yellow, five-pointed star shape, tiny star-shaped freckles, dreamy eyes, soft edge glow. Glow intensifies as night deepens (Shots 7-10). The sad businessman = wrinkled suit, loosened tie, tired eyes softening across his scene.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, twilight-to-starry-night palette, dreamy glow" in every prompt. Progress lighting: dusk lavender → cloud-white silver → twilight purple → deep midnight blue with stars.
- **Audio:** Yángmáo's voice soft, dreamy, poetic. The sad man's voice tired at first, warming when he laughs. Narrator voice sleepy and reverent, especially for the star-alignment beat.
- **Music:** Ambient strings and soft piano throughout, celestial chimes during Point reveals (Shots 3, 4, 6, 7), swelling awe during Shot 10, quiet lullaby for the goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Slow star-wipe dissolves between the five Point moments. Very long, dreamy dissolve from Shot 10 into Shot 11, and into the goodnight.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shots 8-10 (summit/wish/alignment) can be held longer for the emotional climax.
