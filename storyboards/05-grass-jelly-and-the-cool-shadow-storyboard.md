# Storyboard: Story 05 — Grass Jelly and the Cool Shadow

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, deep banyan green and ancient temple ochre palette, dappled shade and warm gold, soft edges, contemplative cool mood

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Caǎo (smooth dark inky black-green cube, tiny black beanie, calm half-lidded eyes, faint herbal mint-green aura) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots. Her cool aura should subtly cool the temperature of every frame.

---

## Shot 1 — CHILL UNDER THE BANYAN (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A small dark inky black-green grass jelly cube wearing a tiny black beanie sitting calmly under a four-hundred-year-old banyan tree in a Tainan temple courtyard, reading a small poetry book. A subtle herbal mint-green aura around her makes the air visibly cooler. Peaceful storybook illustration with deep green and warm ochre tones.`

**Narration:**
> "While the other desserts in Taiwan bounced, stretched, and made spectacles of themselves, Caǎo sat in the corner of a temple courtyard in Tainan, wearing a tiny black beanie, reading poetry, and radiating an aura of such profound chill that the temperature around her was literally two degrees cooler than the rest of the city. 'Chill,' she said to no one. 'I've got this.'"

## Shot 2 — TAINAN'S TEMPLES (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Wide establishing shot of Tainan — ancient ornate temples with curved roofs and dragon sculptures, hundred-year-old banyan trees, stone courtyards, an old man playing chess, a cat sleeping in a sunlit courtyard. Atmospheric storybook illustration with deep ochre, green, and warm gold tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Caǎo lived in Táinán, the oldest city in Taiwan and, in her objective opinion, the best. Tainan had hundreds of temples — ornate, colorful, centuries-old buildings with dragon sculptures and stone courtyards where old men played chess and cats slept in pools of sunlight."

## Shot 6 — THE QUIET CONVERSATION (1:25–1:45)

**Visual Prompt:**

The grass jelly's poetry book lies open between them. Warm dappled afternoon light through the leaves. Heartwarming storybook illustration with green, gold, and yellow.`

**Narration:**

## Shot 7 — SUPERPOWER OR SAD? (1:45–2:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Close-up of a small dark grass jelly cube in a beanie speaking earnestly to a young girl, her calm half-lidded eyes warm now. A bowl of shaved ice with grass jelly cubes on top floating in the background as a thought bubble. Wise, intimate storybook illustration with green and amber.`

**Narration:**
> "'Grass jelly is never the headliner. You don't say give me a bowl of grass jelly. You say give me shaved ice WITH grass jelly. It's always the WITH. The thing that makes everything else better.' 'That sounds kind of sad.' 'Does it? Or does it sound like a superpower?'"

## Shot 9 — THE JUDGE'S VERDICT (2:20–2:40)

**Narration:**

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Caǎo = smooth dark inky black-green cube, tiny black beanie, calm half-lidded eyes, subtle herbal mint-green aura. Always cool, deadpan. Girl in yellow sundress = ~7 years old, quiet thoughtful eyes. Banyan tree = enormous ancient roots, dappled green canopy.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, banyan green and temple ochre, dappled shade" in every prompt. Festival shots (3-4, 9) introduce neon brightness for contrast.
- **Audio:** Caǎo's voice low-key, deadpan, dry. Girl's voice small and quiet. Festival sequence noisy and chaotic, then sudden silence into the courtyard scenes.
- **Music:** Soft lo-fi/herbal ambient throughout, contrasting sharply with pop/electronic during the festival shots. Cool and contemplative for the courtyard scenes.
- **Transitions:** Slow dissolves throughout. Hard cut from chaotic Shot 4 to quiet Shot 5 to emphasize the shift.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 6 (quiet conversation) and Shot 8 (reading poetry together) deserve longer holds for emotional weight.
