# Storyboard: Story 24 — Mochi and the Demon Slumber Party

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, warm misty hot spring atmosphere, soft moonlight, cozy and heartwarming tones

> **Tip:** Generate reference images of Mochi (round white blob, potato starch dust, rosy pink cheeks, stretchy taffy arms) and the demon kids (knee-high, round-bellied, stubby-horned, various colors) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots.

---

## Shot 1 — MAXIMUM RELAXATION MODE (0:00–0:12)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A round, soft, white mochi character with rosy pink cheeks and potato starch dusting sitting peacefully at the edge of a steaming outdoor hot spring pool at night. Mountains and cedar trees in the background, moonlight, gentle steam rising. Wulai, Taiwan setting. Serene, cozy storybook illustration, warm tones.`

**Narration:**
> "The steam from the Wulai hot springs smelled like warm rocks and sleepy mountains, and Mochi was having the time of her soft, pillowy life. 'This is the COZIEST night in the HISTORY of nights!'"

---

## Shot 2 — DEMON KIDS CRASH IN (0:12–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Twelve tiny colorful demon children tumbling out of a dark forest into a hot spring clearing — crashing into each other, bonking into a lantern post, rolling in a wailing pile. Stubby horns, round bellies, various colors (blue, red, purple, yellow, green). Their tears sizzle on the ground leaving scorch marks. Comedic chaos, warm storybook illustration, misty mountain setting.`

**Narration:**
> "And then twelve demon children crashed out of the forest. Tiny, round-bellied, stubby-horned little demons, every single one either crying, shivering, or both. Their tears made a sizzling sound when they hit the ground. Adorable? Absolutely. A minor fire hazard? Also yes."

---

## Shot 3 — THE MEGA HUG (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A white mochi character stretching her taffy-like arms impossibly wide to embrace all twelve tiny demon children at once in an enormous group hug beside a steamy hot spring. The demons look surprised and comforted. Arms wrap around like ribbons of dough. Warm moonlight, misty mountains, heartwarming storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "'COME HERE RIGHT NOW AND LET ME HUG YOU!!!' She launched herself and stretched — and stretched — and STRETCHED. Her arms swooped to scoop up all twelve in one spectacular, physics-defying embrace. 'Nobody panic — Auntie Mochi is HERE!'"

---

## Shot 4 — HOT SPRING CHAOS (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Twelve tiny demon children in a hot spring pool having the time of their lives — one sinking in up to its horns sighing blissfully, one sniffing the water suspiciously, one drinking it, and one on a rock mid-cannonball creating a massive splash. A mochi character stretches into an umbrella shape over the smallest demons. Comedic, warm storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "'Is this soup?' 'It's NOT soup.' A purple demon took a big slurp. 'Tastes like soup.' A dumpling-shaped demon yelled 'WATCH THIS!' and executed a cannonball so spectacular it sent a wave crashing over everyone."

---

## Shot 5 — PEACEFUL SOAK (1:10–1:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Twelve tiny demon children soaking peacefully in a moonlit hot spring, steam curling upward, stars visible through mist. A white mochi character sits at the pool edge with stretchy arms draped around as many demon kids as possible. Mountains and cedar trees silhouetted against a starry sky. Serene, dreamy storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "For a few minutes, everything was peaceful. Steam curled from the water. The mountains stood dark and quiet against a sky full of stars. Mochi sat with her arms around as many demon kids as she could reach — which was all of them."

## Shot 6 — "I'M HUNGRY!" (1:25–1:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Twelve tiny demon children bouncing in a hot spring, mouths open, chanting, tiny horns bobbing, waves sloshing over the edges. Speech bubbles or visual energy showing excitement. A mochi character looks at them with wide eyes and a growing grin. Energetic, fun storybook illustration, warm tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'Auntie Mochi? I'm hungry.' 'I want mochi ice cream! That's what we were gonna have at our slumber party!' The chant started: 'MO-CHI ICE CREAM! MO-CHI ICE CREAM!' All twelve bouncing in the spring."

**Narration:**

## Shot 8 — THE CONVENIENCE STORE STRETCH (1:50–2:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A white mochi character stretching one impossibly long taffy arm three blocks down a mountain road to reach into a glowing convenience store (7-Eleven style) and grab ice cream cups. The arm stretches through the misty night like a long white ribbon. Hot springs in the foreground, convenience store glowing in the distance. Whimsical, funny storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**

## Shot 9 — MOCHI ICE CREAM ASSEMBLY (2:05–2:20)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A translucent, pizza-dough-thin sheet of white mochi dough glowing in moonlight, wrapping around colorful scoops of ice cream (strawberry pink, mango gold, taro purple, red bean burgundy) on warm stones beside a hot spring. Potato starch drifts like fairy dust. Twelve demon children watch with enormous sparkling eyes. Magical, whimsical storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "She stretched herself thin — pizza-dough thin — a sheet of beautiful pillowy mochi dough glowing in the moonlight. And she wrapped. Strawberry — smoosh. Vanilla — smoosh. Twelve perfect balls of mochi ice cream, each one a tiny edible hug."

**Narration:**

## Shot 11 — THE SLEEPY PILE (2:35–2:55)

**Visual Prompt:**

Steam curls gently upward. Stars visible through mist. A shimmering spirit-world portal glows faintly above. Ultra-cozy, dreamy storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (2:55–3:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A round, full, pillowy white mochi character sitting alone by a steamy moonlit hot spring, fully puffed back to normal shape, staring up at stars with a contented smile. Misty mountains, gentle steam, soft starlight. Peaceful, warm, sleepy storybook illustration fading to soft darkness.`

**Narration:**
> "'I got to HUG ice cream AND demon babies in the SAME NIGHT. BEST. NIGHT. EVER!!!' Love stretches to fit everyone. Goodnight, little one. Auntie Mochi would absolutely hug you right now if she could. Sweet dreams."

---

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Mochi = round white blob, potato starch dust, rosy pink cheeks, stretchy taffy arms. Demon kids = knee-high, various colors, stubby horns, round bellies.
- **Energy arc:** Peaceful opening → chaotic arrival → warm nurturing → silly hot spring antics → sweet ice cream scene → sleepy cozy ending.
- **Audio:** Mochi's voice should be warm, enthusiastic, lots of exclamation energy. Demon kids = high-pitched, giggly.
- **Music:** Gentle lullaby for opening/closing, playful xylophone for the chaos scenes, soft piano for the sleepy pile.
- **Transitions:** Soft dissolves throughout. Slightly longer hold on the sleepy pile shot.
