# Storyboard: Story 20 — The Grand Dessert Reunion

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, grand celebration palette — warm gold, crimson, paper-lantern rainbow, Taipei midnight blue — soft edges, cozy-epic lighting

> **Tip:** This is an ensemble finale featuring ALL 19 main characters plus 4 guests. Generate a master reference sheet in Shot 3 with each dessert character in their signature pose/look (see Production Notes). Attach the reference sheet to every subsequent shot — character consistency is the biggest challenge in this storyboard.

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency (THE BIG CHALLENGE):** This is the most character-heavy storyboard in the series. Build a master reference sheet before generating any shots. Key looks:
  - **Boba:** glossy black pearl, huge eyes, tiny arms/legs
  - **Taro:** chunky purple oval, reading glasses, dusty lavender
  - **Aiyu:** translucent golden-amber cube, lemon-seed freckles
  - **Mochi:** white pillowy blob, starch dusted, stretchy taffy arms
  - **Caǎo:** dark black-green cube, black beanie, half-lidded eyes
  - **Feng:** golden rectangle in fancy gift-box wrapping
  - **Bīng:** towering shaved ice with colorful toppings, sunglasses
  - **Mango:** bright orange-yellow, sweatband, rosy cheek
  - **Hóng Dòu:** burgundy bean with white stripe, tiny pot/ladle
  - **Tāng Yuán:** round white rice ball, rosy cheeks, sesame core
  - **Dòuhuā:** smooth creamy white, serene half-closed eyes
  - **Lìzhī:** bumpy pink shell, tiny fan, dramatic posture
  - **Bǎi:** wrinkly purple-brown, mischievous grin, orange pulp visible
  - **Dìguā:** rough brown skin, golden-orange interior, hardhat
  - **Yēguǒ:** translucent white cube, flip-flops, shell necklace
  - **Dàn:** golden-brown egg waffle, bottle-cap top hat
  - **Bùdīng:** caramel flan, golden top, dripping caramel, nervous eyes
  - **Zhīma:** golden sesame-coated ball, bold eyes
  - **Yángmáo:** translucent five-pointed starfruit, soft glow
  - **Piper:** Emperor penguin, glittery blue snowboard
  - **Barnaby:** chinstrap penguin, orange pizza-board
  - **Willa:** Little Blue penguin, tiny purple snowboard
  - **Finnegan:** Rockhopper penguin, yellow-orange crest, red fish-sticker board
  - **Uncle Piggle Wiggle:** jolly mustache, patchwork coat, carpet bag
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, warm gold crimson and paper-lantern rainbow" in every prompt. Grand Hotel palace-style interior with dragon columns consistent across all ballroom shots.
- **Audio:** Each character's line should keep their established voice from prior stories. Narrator warm and reverent, especially on Uncle Piggle Wiggle's toast and the goodnight. Consider individual voice snippets for the arrival shots.
- **Music:** Festive Taiwanese pop with traditional instruments for arrivals and dance. Swell to warm emotional strings during Uncle Piggle Wiggle's toast and Boba's speech. Soft lullaby for the terrace finale.
- **Transitions:** Quick dynamic cuts during the arrival montages (Shots 3-7). Slower ceremonial dissolves during the toast and speech. Long, tender fade on Shot 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Arrivals (Shots 3-7) can be split into faster sub-clips if needed. Shot 9 (toast) and Shot 12 (terrace) should run longer for emotional weight.
- **Special note:** If Seedance struggles with 20+ characters in frame, split the dance shot (11) into two faster cuts showing half the cast each. Goal: every character gets visible screen time somewhere in the 3:30 runtime.
