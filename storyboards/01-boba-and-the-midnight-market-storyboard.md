# Storyboard: Story 01 — Boba and the Midnight Market

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, warm neon-and-lantern night market palette — gold, crimson, amber — soft edges, cozy wonder

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Boba (glossy black-brown sphere, huge eyes, tiny arms and legs, leaves a faint sticky trail) in Shot 1. Attach to every subsequent shot. The Shilin Night Market environment should stay consistent across all stall shots.

---

## Shot 1 — INSIDE THE CUP (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny glossy black tapioca pearl with huge expressive eyes pressing her sticky little hands against the foggy plastic wall of a tall milk tea cup. Through the cup, blurry neon lights and red lanterns of a night market glow. Cozy, intimate storybook illustration with warm amber and brown tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Boba had never left the cup. The cup was safe. The cup was familiar. But Boba didn't want straw rides — Boba wanted OUT. 'Let's bounce!' she whispered, pressing her tiny sticky hands against the plastic wall."

---

## Shot 2 — SHILIN NIGHT MARKET (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A wide establishing shot of Shilin Night Market in Taipei at night — endless food stalls, red paper lanterns, neon signs in Chinese, steam rising from grills, thousands of people walking. Vibrant, magical storybook illustration with rich gold, crimson, and warm market light.`

**Narration:**
> "Through the foggy cup she could see lights — thousands of them — strung like jewels across the most magical place in all of Táiwān: the yèshì. Specifically, the Shilin Night Market. The big one. The legend."

---

## Shot 3 — THE TIP (0:30–0:45)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A milk tea cup tipping precariously on a vendor's counter as two arguing customers shout in the background. The plastic lid is popping, the straw falling out, a tiny black tapioca pearl about to escape. Comedic, dramatic storybook illustration with warm market lights.`

**Narration:**
> "The vendor got distracted by an argument over whose drink was more refreshing. ('PAPAYA!' 'MILK TEA!') In the chaos, the cup tipped. The lid popped. And Boba, pressed against the opening, felt warm night air hit her face for the first time. 'Jiāyóu,' she whispered — and she bounced."

---

## Shot 4 — TINY ON THE PAVEMENT (0:45–1:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Ground-level view of a tiny glossy black tapioca pearl lying on wet glittering pavement, surrounded by enormous human feet in flip-flops, sneakers, and high heels stomping past. A shopping bag crashes down nearby like a meteor. Awe-struck terror, storybook illustration with neon reflections in puddles.`

**Narration:**
> "She was the smallest thing in the biggest, loudest, most wonderful place she'd ever seen. And she was absolutely terrified. The ground shook with footsteps. Each foot was the size of a building."

---

## Shot 5 — ROLLING PAST THE STALLS (1:00–1:20)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A small glossy tapioca pearl rolling determinedly past a series of night market stalls — a mountain of golden fried chicken, a game booth with stacked cans, a vendor waving tongs like an orchestra conductor. Motion blur and warm market light. Adventurous storybook illustration.`

**Narration:**
> "She rolled past the first stall: a mountain of fried chicken so golden it looked dipped in sunset. 'Lái lái lái!' the vendor shouted, waving tongs like a conductor. She rolled past a game booth. She rolled past — and stopped."

---

## Shot 6 — GRANDMA'S CART (1:20–1:40)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny ancient-looking food cart with a hand-painted sign reading "阿嬤的珍珠奶茶 — Grandma's Boba Milk Tea." Behind it, the oldest, smallest, most wrinkled woman tenderly rolls cassava-and-brown-sugar dough into perfect tapioca pearls between her palms. A tiny tapioca pearl watches in awe from the ground. Warm, magical storybook illustration with golden lantern light.`

**Narration:**
> "She was making tapioca pearls. By hand. Boba watched, transfixed, as the old woman's fingers — quick and sure — kneaded and rolled each piece into a perfect sphere. 'That's how I was made,' Boba breathed. 'Someone did that for ME.'"

---

## Shot 7 — PINBALL THROUGH THE CROWD (1:40–1:55)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A tiny tapioca pearl pinballing through the night market at high speed — bouncing off shoes, chair legs, cart wheels — flying past a coffin bread stall, a man sculpting sugar dragons, and a woman painting names on grains of rice. Motion lines, comedic chaos, storybook illustration with neon streaks.`

**Narration:**
> "A gust of wind from a giant exhaust fan blew her sideways, and she went tumbling like a tiny bowling ball, ricocheting off shoes and chair legs, catching glimpses of wonders: coffin bread, sugar dragons, names painted on grains of rice."

---

## Shot 8 — MEETING TARO (1:55–2:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A small tapioca pearl crashed into a chunky purple taro chunk wearing tiny round reading glasses, sitting calmly on the edge of a dessert display. The taro is patient and wise; Boba is breathless. Warm dessert shop interior, cozy storybook illustration with purple and gold tones.`

**Narration:**
> "She crashed into something soft. Something purple. Something that said, 'Good things take time to steam.' It was a chunk of taro, sitting calmly with the patience of a mountain. 'You must be new out here.' 'I just escaped my cup,' Boba admitted."

---

## Shot 9 — TARO'S WISDOM (2:15–2:35)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A purple taro chunk in reading glasses and a tiny tapioca pearl sitting together on a counter watching the river of night market people flow past — a little girl tugging her mother's hand, teenagers laughing, an old couple sharing one cup of tea. Warm, contemplative storybook illustration with soft golden light.`

**Narration:**
> "'You don't have to do everything tonight. The night market will be here tomorrow. The brave thing is taking the first bounce.' 'I already took the first bounce.' 'Then you've already done the hardest part.'"

---

## Shot 10 — CHOOSING THE CUP (2:35–2:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A vendor preparing a fresh cup of boba milk tea — creamy tea, ice cubes, empty space at the bottom. A tiny glossy tapioca pearl bounces three magnificent inches through the air, mid-arc, about to land in the cup. Warm, triumphant storybook illustration with golden market light.`

**Narration:**
> "'I'm not stuck in a cup because I've never known anything else. I'm choosing the cup because I've SEEN everything else, and I know where I belong.' She bounced — one glorious, magnificent bounce — and landed with a satisfying PLOP in the fresh cup of milk tea."

---

## Shot 11 — RISING THROUGH THE STRAW (2:50–3:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A close-up view from inside a boba milk tea cup, looking up through creamy tea and ice cubes at a little girl's smiling face above as she sips through a fat boba straw. Lantern light frames her face. Warm, dreamy storybook illustration with amber and cream tones.`

**Narration:**
> "'Xièxie,' the little girl said. She took a long sip, and Boba felt herself rising — up, up through the tea, through the straw, toward the stars and the lanterns and the beautiful, noisy, extraordinary world."

---

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:10–3:25)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A peaceful Shilin Night Market winding down at late hour, lanterns swaying gently, a single cup of boba milk tea sitting on a counter in a shaft of warm light. Sleepy moonlit overlay. Cozy storybook illustration fading into amber and deep blue.`

**Narration:**
> "Tonight, little dreamer, may you be as brave as a tiny tapioca pearl who bounced out of her cup and into the world. The night market will be there tomorrow. Float in the warm, sweet tea of sleep. Goodnight, little boba. Goodnight."

---

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Boba = perfectly round, glossy black-brown sphere, huge expressive eyes, tiny arms and legs, faint sticky trail. Taro = chunky purple oval, lavender swirl, tiny round reading glasses. Old woman = ancient, wrinkled, kind, working hands.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, warm neon and lantern night market" in every prompt. Ground-level POV for Shots 4-5 to emphasize Boba's smallness.
- **Audio:** Boba's voice energetic and punchy. Taro's voice slow, calm, slightly old-fashioned. Warm narrator throughout.
- **Music:** Soft Taiwanese street ambience underneath. Build into market bustle for Shots 5-7, settle into warm strings for the Taro scenes, dreamy lullaby for Shots 11-12.
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts during the pinball sequence (Shot 7). Slow dissolves into Shots 6 (grandma's cart) and 11 (rising through straw). Long fade on Shot 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 6 (grandma making pearls) and Shot 10 (the leap) deserve a beat longer for emotional weight.
