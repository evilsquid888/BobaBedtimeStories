# Storyboard: Story 17 — Pudding and the Wobble Contest

**Platform:** CapCut Video Studio + Seedance 2.0
**Total Shots:** 12
**Estimated Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
**Art Style (use on ALL shots):** Whimsical storybook illustration, golden caramel and soft cream tones, polished marble mall lighting, soft edges, cozy humor

> **Tip:** Generate a reference image of Bùdīng (classic caramel flan shape, golden top, dark caramel dripping unevenly down the sides, wide nervous eyes, tiny anxious hands, mid-wobble) in Shot 1. Attach to all subsequent shots — his wobble and drip pattern should stay visually consistent even as the situation escalates.

---

## Shot 1 — I'M FINE (I'M WOBBLING) (0:00–0:15)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A classic golden caramel pudding shaped like an upside-down cup, dark caramel dripping unevenly down its sides, wide nervous eyes, tiny anxious hands raised. Mid-wobble, slightly tilted. Polished marble mall floor reflecting him. Humorous, warm storybook illustration with golden-amber tones.`

**Narration:**
> "Bùdīng was wobbling, and he wanted everyone to know that he was FINE. 'I'm fine! I'm fine! ...I'm wobbling.' He was always wobbling. That was the problem. Or was it the point?"

---

## Shot 2 — BREEZE CENTER DISPLAY (0:15–0:30)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A fancy patisserie dessert display case on the B1 level of a luxury Taipei mall. Perfect rows of pastel macarons, precise mille-feuille, smooth chocolate truffles — and in the middle, a wobbling golden caramel pudding looking out of place. Marble floors, designer boutique lighting. Elegant storybook illustration with soft pastels and cream.`

**Narration:**
> "Bùdīng lived in the dessert display of a patisserie on the B1 level of Wéifēng Guǎngchǎng — surrounded by cakes that did NOT wobble. Macarons sat in perfect rows. The mille-feuille stood at precise right angles. Bùdīng had none of these advantages."

---

## Shot 3 — CHEF YÁNG FROWNS (0:30–0:50)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A stern, elegant pastry chef in a white chef's coat peering through the glass of a dessert display case, frowning directly at a wobbly golden pudding. The pudding cowers, his caramel dripping a little faster. Perfect desserts surround him, unmoving. Comedic tension storybook illustration with cool mall lighting.`

**Narration:**
> "Chef Yáng paused. 'That pudding is wobbling.' 'I'M FINE!' Bùdīng squeaked. 'The caramel distribution is uneven. We have the mall's Dessert Showcase tomorrow. I need perfection. Not wobble.' Bùdīng felt his caramel go cold."

---

## Shot 4 — PRACTICING STILLNESS (0:50–1:10)

**Visual Prompt:**
`Night in an empty dessert display case lit by dim overnight lighting. A golden caramel pudding standing in the exact center, eyes squeezed shut in concentration, trying to be still. A vent blows cool air and the pudding visibly wobbles two inches to the left, bumping a macaron. Humorous storybook illustration with soft blue moonlight and amber.`

**Narration:**
> "He tried holding his breath. He tried clenching his custard. He tried thinking very firm thoughts, like oak trees and concrete and the tax code. For three seconds, he was perfectly still. Then the air conditioning kicked in and he wobbled two inches to the left."

## Shot 7 — THE GIRL GIGGLES (1:45–2:05)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A six-year-old girl in the mall standing in front of a tiny pedestal, pointing and laughing with pure delight at a golden caramel pudding that is wobbling dramatically. The pudding's eyes are wide with confusion. Warm storybook illustration, soft golden spotlight, the girl's joyful face front and center.`

**Narration:**

## Shot 10 — THE JUDGE'S VERDICT (2:40–3:00)

**Visual Prompt:**
`A stern silver-haired head judge with glasses standing before a tiny pedestal, looking down at a wobbling golden caramel pudding with a slow, reluctant, respectful smile. **Narration:**

## Shot 12 — GOODNIGHT (3:15–3:30)

**Visual Prompt:**

Macarons and cakes gathered around like friends. Chef Yáng smiling through the glass. Soft, sleepy storybook illustration fading into warm amber and cream.`

**Narration:**

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** Bùdīng = classic caramel flan, golden top, dark caramel dripping unevenly, wide nervous eyes, tiny anxious hands, constant subtle wobble. Chef Yáng = elegant woman in white chef's coat, severe expression softening across the story.
- **Art style consistency:** Include "whimsical storybook illustration, golden caramel and cream, polished mall lighting" in every prompt. Shift from cold/sterile mall lighting (Shots 1-6) to warm golden spotlight (Shots 7-12).
- **Audio:** Bùdīng's lines delivered with stuttery anxious energy — every "I'm fine!" should sound a little less convinced.
- **Music:** Delicate classical piano for the fancy-mall scenes, warming to playful strings when the girl laughs, triumphant and warm by the trophy moment, lullaby for the goodnight.
- **Transitions:** Jiggle/wobble transition effect between shots if available in CapCut. Slow dissolves into Shots 7, 11, and 12.
- **Each clip:** 5–15 seconds via Seedance 2.0. Shot 8 (ring of laughter) and Shot 10 (judge's verdict) can run a beat longer for emotional beats.
